## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Trai Dat khong phai la hinh cau hoan hao вЂ” no phinh ra o xich dao khoang 21 km. Su lech nho tuong chung nay chinh la nhieu loan lon nhat doi voi ve tinh o duoi GEO, de dang lam lu mo tat ca cac hieu ung khac cho cac quy dao LEO va MEO.

J2 gay ra hai hieu ung tao ra su troi dat (tuyen tinh, lau dai) ma moi nha thiet ke quy dao phai tinh den:

1. **Tien dong nut** вЂ” mat phang quy dao quay quanh truc cua Trai Dat. Voi ISS ($i = 51.6В°$), mat phang hoan thanh mot vong quay day du moi 72 ngay. Bo qua dieu nay va mo hinh phu song cua chom sao cua ban se sai.

2. **Tien dong diem chua** вЂ” huong cua hinh ellipse quay ben trong mat phang cua no. Voi cac quy dao Molniya, cac ky su co y dat tai do nghieng toi han (63.4В°) de giu diem chua o ban cau bac.

Hai ung dung phu thuoc hoan toan vao J2:
- **Quy dao dong bo mat troi** (du bao thoi tiet/quan sat Trai Dat): khai thac co y su tien dong nut J2 de giu mat phang quy dao thang hang voi Mat Troi.
- **Quy dao lap lai vet dat**: thiet ke do cao sao cho su troi J2 tich luy thanh so nguyen chinh xac cac vet dat moi ngay.

## Truc giac

### Hinh dang Trai Dat va he so J2

The nang trong truong cua Trai Dat khong phai la cua mot vat diem. Thuat ngu hieu chinh chiem uu the la song dieu hoa vong dai thu hai $J_2$:

$$U = \frac{\mu}{r}\left[1 - J_2\left(\frac{R_E}{r}\right)^2 P_2(\sin\phi)\right] + \text{cac thuat ngu bac cao}$$

trong do $P_2(\sin\phi) = \frac{1}{2}(3\sin^2\phi - 1)$ la da thuc Legendre, $\phi$ la vi do tam dia cau.

## Co che hoat dong

### Cong thuc ty le lau dai

Cac ty le lau dai bac mot do J2, lay trung binh tren mot quy dao:

**Troi RAAN** $\dot{\Omega}$:

$$\dot{\Omega} = -\frac{3}{2} J_2 \left(\frac{R_E}{p}\right)^2 n \cos i \quad \text{[rad/s]}$$

**Troi goc so voi diem chua** $\dot{\omega}$:

$$\dot{\omega} = \frac{3}{4} J_2 \left(\frac{R_E}{p}\right)^2 n \left(5\cos^2 i - 1\right) \quad \text{[rad/s]}$$

Trong do:
- $p = a(1-e^2)$ вЂ” ban kinh tiet dien [km]
- $n = \sqrt{\mu/a^3}$ вЂ” chuyen dong trung binh [rad/s]
- $R_E = 6378.137$ km вЂ” ban kinh xich dao Trai Dat
- $J_2 = 1.08263 \times 10^{-3}$

Chuyen doi sang do/ngay (nhan rad/s voi $86400 \times 180/\pi$):

$$\dot{\Omega} \approx -9.964 \left(\frac{R_E}{a}\right)^{7/2} \frac{\cos i}{(1-e^2)^2} \quad \text{[do/ngay]}$$

### Vi du tinh toan

**ISS** ($a = 6778$ km, $e = 0.0006$, $i = 51.6В°$):

$$p = 6778(1 - 0.0006^2) \approx 6778 \text{ km}$$

$$n = \sqrt{398600 / 6778^3} = 1.134 \times 10^{-3} \text{ rad/s}$$

$$\dot{\Omega} = -\frac{3}{2}(1.08263\times10^{-3})\left(\frac{6378}{6778}\right)^2 (1.134\times10^{-3}) \cos(51.6В°)$$

$$= -8.791\times10^{-7} \text{ rad/s} = -5.04\text{ do/ngay}$$

Chu ky tien dong day du: $360/5.04 = 71.4$ ngay.

**Molniya** ($a = 26554$ km, $e = 0.74$, $i = 63.4В°$):

$$p = 26554(1 - 0.74^2) = 26554 \times 0.4524 = 12012 \text{ km}$$

$$\dot{\omega} \propto 5\cos^2(63.4В°) - 1 = 5(0.449)^2 - 1 = 5(0.2016) - 1 = 0.008 \approx 0$$

Tai $i = 63.4В°$, $\dot{\omega} = 0$ chinh xac вЂ” **do nghieng toi han**. Diem chua giu co dinh o ban cau bac. Day la ly do cac quy dao Molniya su dung chinh xac do nghieng nay.

**Thiet ke quy dao dong bo mat troi:**

Troi can thiet: $\dot{\Omega} = +0.9856В°/\text{ngay}$ (khop voi ~1В°/ngay quy dao Trai Dat quanh Mat Troi)

Giai do nghieng tai do cao cho truoc:

$$\cos i = -\frac{\dot{\Omega} \cdot 2p^2}{3 J_2 R_E^2 n}$$

Tai $a = 7078$ km (do cao 700 km), $e = 0$, $p = 7078$ km:

$$n = \sqrt{398600/7078^3} = 1.0647\times10^{-3} \text{ rad/s}$$

$$\cos i = -\frac{(1.991\times10^{-7})(2 \times 7078^2)}{3(1.08263\times10^{-3})(6378^2)(1.0647\times10^{-3})}$$

$$\cos i = -0.1273 \quad \Rightarrow \quad i = 97.32В°$$

Do nghieng nguoc chieu nay ($>90В°$) tao ra su troi RAAN theo huong dong can thiet de theo doi Mat Troi.

### J2 o GEO va xa hon

Ngay ca o GEO ($a = 42164$ km), J2 cung khong bang khong:

$$\dot{\Omega}_{GEO} \approx -9.964(6378/42164)^{7/2}\cos(0В°) \approx -0.013В°/\text{ngay}$$

Trong mot nam: $0.013 \times 365 = 4.7В°$ su troi RAAN вЂ” du lon de yeu cau cac hiieu chinh giu tram dong-tay.

Voi cac ve tinh GEO co do nghieng khac khong, J2 cung tao ra dao dong kinh do hang ngay (kieu "so tam", hinh chua 8 khi ve vet phu song ve tinh).

## Ap dung

**Cong cu tinh nhieu loan J2:**

```python
import numpy as np

# Hang so
R_E = 6378.137      # km, ban kinh xich dao Trai Dat
J2  = 1.08263e-3    # khong co don vi
MU  = 398600.4418   # km^3/s^2

def j2_raan_rate(a_km: float, e: float, i_deg: float) -> float:
    """
    Ty le troi RAAN do J2 [do/ngay].
    Am = huong tay (quy dao thuan chieu).
    """
    i = np.radians(i_deg)
    p = a_km * (1 - e**2)
    n = np.sqrt(MU / a_km**3)
    omega_dot = -1.5 * J2 * (R_E/p)**2 * n * np.cos(i)
    return np.degrees(omega_dot) * 86400

def j2_aop_rate(a_km: float, e: float, i_deg: float) -> float:
    """
    Ty le troi goc diem chua do J2 [do/ngay].
    Bang khong tai do nghieng toi han 63.4В°/116.6В°.
    """
    i = np.radians(i_deg)
    p = a_km * (1 - e**2)
    n = np.sqrt(MU / a_km**3)
    omega_dot = 0.75 * J2 * (R_E/p)**2 * n * (5*np.cos(i)**2 - 1)
    return np.degrees(omega_dot) * 86400

def sun_sync_inclination(a_km: float, e: float = 0.0) -> float:
    """
    Do nghieng can thiet cho quy dao dong bo mat troi [do].
    Tra ve do nghieng nguoc chieu (> 90В°).
    """
    p = a_km * (1 - e**2)
    n = np.sqrt(MU / a_km**3)
    req_rad_per_s = np.radians(0.9856) / 86400
    cos_i = -2.0 * req_rad_per_s * p**2 / (3 * J2 * R_E**2 * n)
    if abs(cos_i) > 1:
        raise ValueError(f"Khong co nghiem dong bo mat troi tai a={a_km} km")
    return np.degrees(np.arccos(cos_i))

# --- Bang cac quy dao pho bien ---
print("Ty le nhieu loan J2\n" + "="*65)
print(f"{'Quy dao':<22} {'a[km]':>7} {'e':>6} {'i[do]':>7} "
      f"{'dO/dt[d/ngay]':>14} {'dw/dt[d/ngay]':>14}")
print("-"*65)
truong_hop = [
    ("ISS",           6778,  0.0006, 51.6),
    ("Sentinel-2",    7178,  0.001,  98.62),
    ("GPS (MEO)",     26560, 0.005,  55.0),
    ("Molniya",       26554, 0.740,  63.4),
    ("GEO",           42164, 0.0002,  0.1),
]
for ten, a, e, i in truong_hop:
    dr = j2_raan_rate(a, e, i)
    da = j2_aop_rate(a, e, i)
    print(f"{ten:<22} {a:>7.0f} {e:>6.4f} {i:>7.1f} {dr:>14.4f} {da:>14.4f}")

# --- Bang thiet ke dong bo mat troi ---
print("\nDo nghieng dong bo mat troi vs Do cao\n" + "="*40)
for do_cao in [400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900]:
    a = R_E + do_cao
    i_ss = sun_sync_inclination(a)
    dr = j2_raan_rate(a, 0, i_ss)
    print(f"  Do cao {do_cao:4d} km:  i = {i_ss:.2f}В°  dO/dt = {dr:+.4f}В°/ngay")
```

## Nhung bay an

1. **Quy uoc dau cho troi RAAN.** Quy dao thuan chieu ($i < 90В°$) co $\dot{\Omega} < 0$ (troi ve huong tay). Quy dao nguoc chieu ($i > 90В°$) co $\dot{\Omega} > 0$ (tien dong ve huong dong). Dong bo mat troi khai thac ty le duong cua quy dao nguoc chieu nhe.

2. **Do nghieng toi han la 63.435В°, khong phai 63.4В°.** Voi thiet ke Molniya chinh xac, $5\cos^2 i - 1 = 0$ cho $\cos i = 1/\sqrt{5}$, nen $i = \arccos(1/\sqrt{5}) = 63.435В°$. Goc bu $116.565В°$ cung cho troi AoP bang khong.

3. **Ty le J2 la trung binh bac mot.** Cac cong thuc nay gia dinh lay trung binh tren mot quy dao. Voi cac khoang tich phan ngan hoac quy dao co do lech tam cao gan diem chua, cac nhieu loan tuc thoi khac dang ke voi trung binh lau dai.

4. **Cac song dieu hoa vong dai bac cao quan trong khi can do chinh xac.** Chi J2 cho do chinh xac ~1% voi LEO. De du bao quy dao duoi met (GPS, GRACE), can bao gom J3 den J6 va cac song dieu hoa tiet dien.

5. **Dao dong ngan ky chong len tren troi lau dai.** RAAN thuc su dao dong voi chu ky quy dao dat len tren troi lau dai. Cac tham so osculating (tu TLE) bao gom cac dao dong nay; cac tham so trung binh (dung trong cac cong thuc tren) da loai bo chung.

6. **GEO khong mien nhiem J2.** O 35786 km, $\dot{\Omega} \approx -0.013В°/\text{ngay}$ va $\dot{\omega} \approx +0.018В°/\text{ngay}$. Trong vong doi 15 nam cua ve tinh, troi RAAN khong duoc hieu chinh vuot 70В° вЂ” yeu cau cac co dong Dong-Tay thuong xuyen.

7. **Do lech tam cung thay doi do J2.** J2 gay ra cac dao dong dai ky trong $e$ va $i$ lien ket qua $\omega$. Voi cac quy dao Molniya dong bang tai do nghieng toi han, do lech tam van dao dong tru khi dieu kien quy dao dong bang day du duoc ap dung.

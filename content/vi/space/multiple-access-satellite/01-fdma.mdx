## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

FDMA la phuong phap da truy cap lau doi nhat va don gian nhat. Moi nguoi dung co mot phan tan so rieng — giong nhu cac dai radio rieng biet tren AM/FM. No van la phuong phap chu dao cho truyen hinh ve tinh truyen thong (mot song mang moi kenh) va VSAT hang hai. Hieu FDMA la nen tang de hieu tai sao chung ta phat minh TDMA, CDMA, va MF-TDMA de vuot qua cac han che cua no.

## Ap dung

**May tinh dung luong va hieu qua FDMA:**

```python
import numpy as np

def dung_luong_fdma(bw_transponder_mhz, bw_song_mang_mhz, dai_bao_ve_mhz):
    """
    Tinh dung luong he thong FDMA.
    
    Args:
        bw_transponder_mhz: Tong bang thong transponder (MHz)
        bw_song_mang_mhz: Bang thong moi song mang (MHz)
        dai_bao_ve_mhz: Dai bao ve giua cac song mang (MHz)
    
    Returns:
        Dict voi cac chi so dung luong
    """
    bw_song_mang_hieu_dung = bw_song_mang_mhz + dai_bao_ve_mhz
    so_song_mang = int(bw_transponder_mhz / bw_song_mang_hieu_dung)
    bw_su_dung = so_song_mang * bw_song_mang_mhz
    hieu_qua = bw_su_dung / bw_transponder_mhz
    
    return {
        "so_song_mang": so_song_mang,
        "bw_su_dung_mhz": bw_su_dung,
        "bw_lang_phi_mhz": bw_transponder_mhz - bw_su_dung,
        "hieu_qua": hieu_qua
    }

def ty_so_song_mang_dieu_che(lui_vao_db, lui_ra_db):
    """
    Uoc tinh ty so C/IM dua tren do lui bo khuech dai.
    
    Quy tac: C/IM cai thien ~2 dB moi dB lui ra
    """
    cim_co_ban = 15  # dB tai bao hoa
    cai_thien_cim = 2 * lui_ra_db
    return cim_co_ban + cai_thien_cim

def ngan_sach_lien_ket_fdma(eirp_dbw, ton_hao_duong_db, gt_dbk, bw_song_mang_hz,
                            lui_db, c_im_db, c_n_duong_len_db):
    """
    Tinh ngan sach lien ket FDMA bao gom hieu ung dieu che chenh.
    """
    k_dbw = -228.6  # Hang so Boltzmann theo dBW/K/Hz
    
    c_n0_duong_xuong = eirp_dbw - ton_hao_duong_db + gt_dbk - k_dbw - lui_db
    
    c_n_duong_xuong = c_n0_duong_xuong - 10 * np.log10(bw_song_mang_hz)
    
    c_n_duong_len_tuyen = 10**(c_n_duong_len_db/10)
    c_n_duong_xuong_tuyen = 10**(c_n_duong_xuong/10)
    c_im_tuyen = 10**(c_im_db/10)
    
    c_n_tong = 1 / (1/c_n_duong_len_tuyen + 1/c_n_duong_xuong_tuyen + 1/c_im_tuyen)
    c_n_tong_db = 10 * np.log10(c_n_tong)
    
    return {
        "c_n0_duong_xuong_dbhz": c_n0_duong_xuong,
        "c_n_duong_xuong_db": c_n_duong_xuong,
        "c_n_tong_db": c_n_tong_db
    }
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Bo qua dieu che chenh.** Nhieu song mang qua mot bo khuech dai tao san pham IM. Ban phai lui bo khuech dai, mat 3-6 dB dung luong.

2. **Quen dai bao ve.** Bo loc thuc khong phai hinh chu nhat. Danh 5-10% cho dai bao ve hoac ban se bi nhieu kenh ke.

3. **Gia dinh bo khuech dai tuyen tinh.** TWTA ve tinh phi tuyen gan bao hoa. Dieu nay thay doi moi thu cho FDMA nhieu song mang.

4. **Gan toan bo bang thong cho nguoi dung theo cum.** FDMA gan bang thong vinh vien. Neu luu luong theo cum, 90% transponder nhan roi.

5. **Tron kich thuoc song mang bat can.** Song mang kich thuoc khac nhau co yeu cau lui khac nhau. Song mang lon nhat chi phoi dieu che chenh.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

CDMA cho phep moi nguoi truyen dong thoi tren cung tan so, phan tach bang cac ma duy nhat — giong nhu nhieu cuoc tro chuyen tai mot bua tiec, moi nguoi noi mot ngon ngu khac. SCPC nguoc lai: mot song mang rieng cho moi lien ket, khong chia se. Ca hai deu co vi tri. GPS dung CDMA (tin hieu yeu, suy giam tao nha). Uplink TV quoc te dung SCPC (thong luong toi da, bang thong dam bao). Biet khi nao dung moi cai phan biet ky su ve tinh gioi va xuat sac.

```python
import numpy as np

def do_loi_xu_ly_cdma(toc_do_chip_mcps, toc_do_du_lieu_kbps):
    """
    Tinh do loi xu ly CDMA.
    
    Args:
        toc_do_chip_mcps: Toc do chip (Mcps - megachip moi giay)
        toc_do_du_lieu_kbps: Toc do du lieu (kbps)
    
    Returns:
        Do loi xu ly theo dB
    """
    chip_moi_bit = (toc_do_chip_mcps * 1e6) / (toc_do_du_lieu_kbps * 1e3)
    do_loi_xu_ly_db = 10 * np.log10(chip_moi_bit)
    
    return {
        "chip_moi_bit": chip_moi_bit,
        "do_loi_xu_ly_db": do_loi_xu_ly_db
    }

def dung_luong_cdma(bang_thong_mhz, toc_do_du_lieu_kbps, eb_n0_yeu_cau_db,
                    he_so_hoat_dong_thoai=1.0, do_loi_phan_vung=1.0):
    """
    Uoc tinh dung luong he thong CDMA (mo hinh don gian).
    """
    toc_do_chip_mcps = bang_thong_mhz * 0.8  # Gan dung
    pg = do_loi_xu_ly_cdma(toc_do_chip_mcps, toc_do_du_lieu_kbps)
    
    do_loi_xu_ly_tuyen = 10**(pg["do_loi_xu_ly_db"]/10)
    eb_n0_yeu_cau_tuyen = 10**(eb_n0_yeu_cau_db/10)
    
    so_nguoi_dung = (do_loi_xu_ly_tuyen / eb_n0_yeu_cau_tuyen) * \
                    he_so_hoat_dong_thoai * do_loi_phan_vung
    
    return {
        "do_loi_xu_ly_db": pg["do_loi_xu_ly_db"],
        "so_nguoi_dung_uoc_tinh": int(so_nguoi_dung),
        "chip_moi_bit": pg["chip_moi_bit"]
    }

def hieu_qua_lien_ket_scpc(bw_song_mang_mhz, toc_do_ky_hieu_msps,
                           bit_moi_ky_hieu, ty_le_ma):
    """
    Tinh hieu qua pho lien ket SCPC.
    """
    toc_do_tho_mbps = toc_do_ky_hieu_msps * bit_moi_ky_hieu
    toc_do_thuc_mbps = toc_do_tho_mbps * ty_le_ma
    hieu_qua_pho = toc_do_thuc_mbps / bw_song_mang_mhz
    
    return {
        "toc_do_tho_mbps": toc_do_tho_mbps,
        "toc_do_thuc_mbps": toc_do_thuc_mbps,
        "hieu_qua_pho_bps_hz": hieu_qua_pho
    }

def lap_day_transponder_scpc(bw_transponder_mhz, song_mang):
    """
    Tinh su dung transponder voi nhieu song mang SCPC.
    
    Args:
        bw_transponder_mhz: Tong bang thong transponder
        song_mang: Danh sach cac tuple (bw_song_mang_mhz, bao_ve_mhz)
    
    Returns:
        Chi so su dung
    """
    tong_dung = sum(bw + bv for bw, bv in song_mang)
    su_dung = tong_dung / bw_transponder_mhz
    so_song_mang = len(song_mang)
    
    return {
        "so_song_mang": so_song_mang,
        "tong_dung_mhz": tong_dung,
        "con_lai_mhz": bw_transponder_mhz - tong_dung,
        "su_dung": su_dung
    }
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Van de gan-xa trong CDMA.** May phat gan manh co the nhan chim may phat xa yeu. Dieu khien cong suat la can thiet — khong co no, CDMA that bai.

2. **Gia dinh CDMA luon tiet kiem bang thong.** Do loi xu ly cua CDMA den tu chi phi mo rong bang thong. No thang khi chong nhieu quan trong hon thong luong tho.

3. **Bo qua su don gian cua SCPC.** Modem SCPC re hon, tin cay hon, va khong can dinh thoi mang. Cho lien ket diem-diem rieng, chung thuong thang truy cap chia se.

4. **Quen dai bao ve cho SCPC.** Bo loc thuc co do nghieng. Danh 5-10% giua cac song mang hoac ban se bi nhieu kenh ke.

5. **Uoc tinh qua cao dung luong CDMA.** Dung luong ly thuyet gia dinh dieu khien cong suat hoan hao va ma truc giao. He thong thuc dat 60-70% toi da ly thuyet.

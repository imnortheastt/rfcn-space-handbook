## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

ULA mua dong co tu Aerojet Rocketdyne, vo bao ve tu RUAG, va dien tu hang khong tu Honeywell — moi nha cung cap them bien do va thoi gian giao hang. SpaceX che tao dong co Merlin, dong co Raptor, vo bao ve, dien tu hang khong, may tinh bay, va tham chi noi that van phong noi bo. Ket qua: chi phi moi kg len quy dao cua SpaceX thap hon ULA 5 lan. Starlink tien xa hon — SpaceX che tao ve tinh, phong chung tren ten lua cua chinh ho, va ban internet truc tiep cho nguoi tieu dung. Khong co trung gian. Hieu tich hop doc cho ban biet cong ty khong gian nao co loi the chi phi cau truc va cong ty nao bi ket trong viec tra markup nha cung cap mai mai.

## Ap dung

**Phan tich kinh te tich hop doc:**

```python
import numpy as np

def chong_bien_do(chi_phi_goc, bien_do):
    """
    Tinh gia cuoi cung voi bien do nha cung cap chong chat.
    
    Args:
        chi_phi_goc: Chi phi san xuat goc
        bien_do: Danh sach ty le bien do tai moi cap [0.25, 0.20, 0.15]
    
    Returns:
        Gia cuoi cung sau khi ap dung tat ca bien do
    """
    gia = chi_phi_goc
    for bd in bien_do:
        gia = gia * (1 + bd)
    return gia

def tiet_kiem_tich_hop(chi_phi_thue_ngoai, so_lop_bien_do, chi_phi_noi_bo=0.10):
    """
    Tinh tiet kiem tu tich hop doc.
    
    Args:
        chi_phi_thue_ngoai: Chi phi ban tra nha cung cap
        so_lop_bien_do: So tang nha cung cap
        chi_phi_noi_bo: Chi phi them khi tu lam
    
    Returns:
        Dict voi so sanh chi phi
    """
    bien_do_tb = 0.20  # Bien do nha CC hang khong vu tru dien hinh
    
    chi_phi_goc_uoc_tinh = chi_phi_thue_ngoai / ((1 + bien_do_tb) ** so_lop_bien_do)
    chi_phi_noi = chi_phi_goc_uoc_tinh * (1 + chi_phi_noi_bo)
    tiet_kiem = chi_phi_thue_ngoai - chi_phi_noi
    
    return {
        "thue_ngoai": chi_phi_thue_ngoai,
        "chi_phi_goc_uoc_tinh": chi_phi_goc_uoc_tinh,
        "chi_phi_noi_bo": chi_phi_noi,
        "tiet_kiem": tiet_kiem,
        "tiet_kiem_phan_tram": (tiet_kiem / chi_phi_thue_ngoai) * 100
    }

def so_sanh_chi_phi_phong(tai_trong_kg, chi_phi_noi_bo, gia_ben_ngoai):
    """
    So sanh chi phi phong noi bo vs ben ngoai.
    """
    noi_moi_kg = chi_phi_noi_bo / tai_trong_kg
    ngoai_moi_kg = gia_ben_ngoai / tai_trong_kg
    
    return {
        "tong_noi": chi_phi_noi_bo,
        "tong_ngoai": gia_ben_ngoai,
        "noi_moi_kg": noi_moi_kg,
        "ngoai_moi_kg": ngoai_moi_kg,
        "ti_le_chi_phi": gia_ben_ngoai / chi_phi_noi_bo
    }

def loi_the_tich_hop_chom_sao(so_ve_tinh, chi_phi_vt_noi, chi_phi_vt_ngoai,
                              chi_phi_phong_noi, chi_phi_phong_ngoai,
                              vt_moi_lan_phong):
    """
    Tinh tong loi the chi phi cua tich hop doc cho chom sao.
    """
    so_lan_phong = np.ceil(so_ve_tinh / vt_moi_lan_phong)
    
    tong_noi = (so_ve_tinh * chi_phi_vt_noi) + (so_lan_phong * chi_phi_phong_noi)
    tong_ngoai = (so_ve_tinh * chi_phi_vt_ngoai) + (so_lan_phong * chi_phi_phong_ngoai)
    
    return {
        "tong_noi": tong_noi,
        "tong_ngoai": tong_ngoai,
        "tiet_kiem": tong_ngoai - tong_noi,
        "tiet_kiem_phan_tram": ((tong_ngoai - tong_noi) / tong_ngoai) * 100,
        "ti_le_chi_phi": tong_ngoai / tong_noi
    }
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Danh gia thap NRE.** Tu che tao can R&D, dung cu, va co so tra truoc. Chi co loi o khoi luong.

2. **Tich hop linh kien khong chien luoc.** Tu lam bu long lang phi ky thuat vao hang hoa.

3. **Bo qua chi phi co hoi.** Ky su lam ban khong lam ten lua. Do tong nang suat.

4. **Gia dinh bien do nha CC la loi nhuan thuan.** Nha CC co chuyen mon ban phai xay dung. Tinh chi phi hoc tap.

5. **Bo qua rui ro chuoi cung ung.** Tich hop giam rui ro ben ngoai nhung tap trung rui ro noi bo. Mot vu chay nha may dung tat ca.

6. **Tich hop qua som.** Startup nen mua cho den khi khoi luong bien minh tu lam. Dung xay nha may truoc product-market fit.

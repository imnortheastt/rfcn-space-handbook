## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Mot ve tinh truyen thong GEO truyen thong ton $200-500M va mat 3-5 nam de che tao. Mot ve tinh Starlink ton duoi $250k va SpaceX che tao 6 chiec moi ngay. Do la giam 1000x chi phi va tang 100x toc do. Bi mat khong phai la cong nghe tot hon — ma la triet ly san xuat. SpaceX, Planet, va OneWeb doi xu voi ve tinh nhu dien tu tieu dung: thiet ke chuan hoa, linh kien COTS, day chuyen lap rap, va chap nhan mot so don vi hong thay vi hoan hao tung chiec. Hieu kinh te san xuat dai tra cho ban biet doanh nghiep khong gian nao se thang.

```python
import numpy as np

def chi_phi_duong_cong_hoc_tap(chi_phi_don_vi_dau, so_don_vi, ty_le_hoc_tap=0.80):
    """
    Tinh chi phi don vi dua tren duong cong hoc tap Wright.
    
    Args:
        chi_phi_don_vi_dau: Chi phi don vi dau tien san xuat
        so_don_vi: Don vi thu may trong chuoi san xuat
        ty_le_hoc_tap: Ti le chi phi sau khi san xuat gap doi (0.80 = giam 20%)
    
    Returns:
        Chi phi cua don vi thu n
    """
    b = np.log(ty_le_hoc_tap) / np.log(2)
    return chi_phi_don_vi_dau * (so_don_vi ** b)

def chi_phi_tich_luy(chi_phi_don_vi_dau, tong_don_vi, ty_le_hoc_tap=0.80):
    """Tinh tong chi phi san xuat N don vi."""
    chi_phi = [chi_phi_duong_cong_hoc_tap(chi_phi_don_vi_dau, n+1, ty_le_hoc_tap) 
               for n in range(tong_don_vi)]
    return sum(chi_phi)

def chi_phi_don_vi_trung_binh(chi_phi_don_vi_dau, tong_don_vi, ty_le_hoc_tap=0.80):
    """Tinh chi phi trung binh moi don vi trong lan san xuat."""
    return chi_phi_tich_luy(chi_phi_don_vi_dau, tong_don_vi, ty_le_hoc_tap) / tong_don_vi

def cong_suat_day_chuyen(thoi_gian_takt_phut, so_tram, so_ca_ngay, gio_moi_ca=8):
    """
    Tinh cong suat san xuat hang ngay.
    
    Args:
        thoi_gian_takt_phut: Thoi gian moi tram tinh bang phut
        so_tram: So tram lap rap
        so_ca_ngay: So ca lam viec
        gio_moi_ca: So gio moi ca
    
    Returns:
        San luong toi da hang ngay
    """
    phut_moi_ngay = so_ca_ngay * gio_moi_ca * 60
    return phut_moi_ngay / thoi_gian_takt_phut

def tiet_kiem_cots_vs_cap_khong_gian(danh_sach_vat_tu, he_so_cap_khong_gian=1000):
    """
    Tinh tiet kiem tu chien luoc linh kien COTS.
    
    Args:
        danh_sach_vat_tu: Dict {linh kien: gia_cots}
        he_so_cap_khong_gian: He so gia cap khong gian trung binh
    
    Returns:
        Tong COTS, tong tuong duong cap khong gian, tiet kiem
    """
    tong_cots = sum(danh_sach_vat_tu.values())
    tong_khong_gian = tong_cots * he_so_cap_khong_gian
    return tong_cots, tong_khong_gian, tong_khong_gian - tong_cots
```

## Cam bay

1. **Danh gia thap NRE.** Ve tinh dau tien ton 10-100x ve tinh thu tram. Lap ngan sach cho viec hoc.

2. **Toi uu qua muc cho chi phi don vi.** Neu ban chi can 50 ve tinh, san xuat dai tra thua san xuat thu cong.

3. **Diem hong don chuoi cung ung.** COTS co nghia la thoi gian giao hang tieu dung — thieu chip dung day ban.

4. **Bo qua thoi gian thu nghiem.** Lap rap co the la 45 phut; thu chuc nang day du them hang gio. Lap ke hoach cho no.

5. **Thiet ke trong khi san xuat.** Moi thay doi ky thuat ton 10x tren san nha may. Dong bang thiet ke.

6. **Gia dinh mo rong tuyen tinh.** San xuat 10x khong co nghia la co so 10x — tai nguyen chung tro thanh nut that.

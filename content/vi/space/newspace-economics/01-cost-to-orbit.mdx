## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Nam 2010, phong 1 kg len LEO ton $54,000 tren Delta IV. Nam 2024, no chi ton $1,500 tren Falcon 9. Muc giam 36 lan do mo khoa toan bo nen kinh te NewSpace: Starlink, Planet, OneWeb, va hang tram startup ve tinh nho ton tai vi phong tro nen co the chi tra duoc. Hieu cac xu huong chi phi len quy dao cho ban biet mo hinh kinh doanh nao hien kha thi, mo hinh nao se kha thi voi Starship, va tai sao hang khong vu tru truyen thong dang chay dua de canh tranh.

## Ap dung

**Tinh toan kinh te phong cua ban:**

```python
import numpy as np

# Co so du lieu phuong tien phong (du lieu 2024)
PHUONG_TIEN = {
    "Falcon 9": {"leo_kg": 22800, "gia_m": 67, "tai_sd": True},
    "Falcon Heavy": {"leo_kg": 63800, "gia_m": 97, "tai_sd": True},
    "Starship": {"leo_kg": 150000, "gia_m": 3, "tai_sd": True},  # Muc tieu
    "Atlas V": {"leo_kg": 18850, "gia_m": 110, "tai_sd": False},
    "Vulcan": {"leo_kg": 27200, "gia_m": 110, "tai_sd": False},
    "Ariane 6": {"leo_kg": 21600, "gia_m": 82, "tai_sd": False},
    "New Glenn": {"leo_kg": 45000, "gia_m": 70, "tai_sd": True},
    "Electron": {"leo_kg": 300, "gia_m": 7.5, "tai_sd": True},
}

def chi_phi_moi_kg(phuong_tien):
    """Tinh $/kg len LEO."""
    pt = PHUONG_TIEN[phuong_tien]
    return (pt["gia_m"] * 1e6) / pt["leo_kg"]

def chi_phi_phong_cho_tai_trong(tai_trong_kg, phuong_tien):
    """Tinh tong chi phi phong cho tai trong cho truoc."""
    pt = PHUONG_TIEN[phuong_tien]
    if tai_trong_kg > pt["leo_kg"]:
        return None  # Tai trong qua nang
    return pt["gia_m"] * 1e6

def chi_phi_rideshare(tai_trong_kg, gia_co_so=5500):
    """Uoc tinh chi phi rideshare (gia dien hinh SpaceX Transporter)."""
    return tai_trong_kg * gia_co_so

def chi_phi_phong_chom_sao(khoi_luong_vt_kg, so_vt, phuong_tien, vt_moi_lan_phong=None):
    """Tinh tong chi phi phong cho mot chom sao."""
    pt = PHUONG_TIEN[phuong_tien]
    if vt_moi_lan_phong is None:
        vt_moi_lan_phong = int(pt["leo_kg"] / khoi_luong_vt_kg)
    
    so_lan_phong = np.ceil(so_vt / vt_moi_lan_phong)
    tong_chi_phi = so_lan_phong * pt["gia_m"] * 1e6
    chi_phi_moi_vt = tong_chi_phi / so_vt
    
    return {
        "so_lan_phong": int(so_lan_phong),
        "vt_moi_lan_phong": vt_moi_lan_phong,
        "tong_chi_phi_m": tong_chi_phi / 1e6,
        "chi_phi_moi_vt_m": chi_phi_moi_vt / 1e6,
    }

print("May tinh Kinh te Chi phi Phong")
print("=" * 70)

# So sanh chi phi moi kg
print("\n1. Chi phi moi kg len LEO (2024)")
print("-" * 70)
print(f"  {'Phuong tien':<15} {'LEO (t)':>12} {'Gia ($M)':>12} {'$/kg':>10}")
print(f"  {'-'*50}")

for ten in sorted(PHUONG_TIEN.keys(), key=lambda x: chi_phi_moi_kg(x)):
    pt = PHUONG_TIEN[ten]
    cpk = chi_phi_moi_kg(ten)
    print(f"  {ten:<15} {pt['leo_kg']/1000:>12.1f} {pt['gia_m']:>12.1f} {cpk:>10,.0f}")

# Vi du kinh te chom sao
print("\n2. Kinh te Phong Chom sao")
print("-" * 70)

kich_ban = [
    ("Giong Starlink", 260, 12000),
    ("Giong OneWeb", 150, 648),
    ("Planet Dove", 5, 400),
    ("Iridium NEXT", 860, 75),
]

for ten, khoi_luong_vt, so_vt in kich_ban:
    print(f"\n  {ten}: {so_vt} ve tinh × {khoi_luong_vt} kg moi chiec")
    
    for phuong_tien in ["Falcon 9", "Ariane 6", "Starship"]:
        ket_qua = chi_phi_phong_chom_sao(khoi_luong_vt, so_vt, phuong_tien)
        if ket_qua:
            print(f"    {phuong_tien:<12}: {ket_qua['so_lan_phong']:3} lan phong, "
                  f"${ket_qua['tong_chi_phi_m']:,.0f}M tong, "
                  f"${ket_qua['chi_phi_moi_vt_m']:.2f}M/vt")

# Xu huong chi phi lich su
print("\n3. Xu huong Chi phi Lich su ($/kg len LEO)")
print("-" * 70)

lich_su = [
    (1981, "Tau con thoi", 54500),
    (2006, "Delta IV Heavy", 47000),
    (2010, "Falcon 9 v1.0", 8500),
    (2016, "Falcon 9 FT", 4100),
    (2018, "Falcon 9 Block 5", 2700),
    (2024, "Falcon 9 Rideshare", 1500),
    (2026, "Starship (muc tieu)", 200),
]

print(f"  {'Nam':<6} {'Phuong tien':<20} {'$/kg':>10} {'Tuong doi voi Shuttle':>22}")
print(f"  {'-'*60}")

co_so = lich_su[0][2]
for nam, phuong_tien, cpk in lich_su:
    tuong_doi = co_so / cpk
    print(f"  {nam:<6} {phuong_tien:<20} {cpk:>10,} {tuong_doi:>21.1f}x re hon")

# Phan tich nguong mo hinh kinh doanh
print("\n4. Nguong Mo hinh Kinh doanh")
print("-" * 70)

nguong = [
    ("Ve tinh phat song GEO", 50000, "Co loi tu thap nien 1960"),
    ("Chom sao khong lo LEO", 5000, "Duoc mo khoa boi Falcon 9"),
    ("Tram vu tru thuong mai", 3000, "Dang tro nen kha thi"),
    ("Du lich vu tru ($100k/cho)", 2000, "Thi truong moi noi"),
    ("San xuat trong khong gian", 500, "Can Starship"),
    ("Dien mat troi khong gian", 100, "Ly thuyet, can <$100/kg"),
]

print(f"  {'Mo hinh Kinh doanh':<30} {'Max $/kg':>10} {'Trang thai':<30}")
print(f"  {'-'*70}")

for mo_hinh, nguong_cp, trang_thai in nguong:
    print(f"  {mo_hinh:<30} {nguong_cp:>10,} {trang_thai:<30}")

# Chi phi nhien lieu vs phan cung
print("\n5. Kiem tra Thuc te Chi phi Nhien lieu")
print("-" * 70)

print("""
  Luong nhien lieu Starship:
  
  Super Heavy: ~3,400 tan CH4/LOX
  Starship:    ~1,200 tan CH4/LOX
  Tong:        ~4,600 tan
  
  Chi phi nhien lieu (xap xi):
  - Methane long: ~$0.50/kg
  - Oxy long:     ~$0.16/kg
  - Ti le tron:   ~3.6:1 (LOX:CH4)
  
  Tong chi phi nhien lieu:
  - CH4: ~1,000 tan × $500/tan = $500,000
  - LOX: ~3,600 tan × $160/tan = $576,000
  - Tong: ~$1.1M moi lan phong
  
  Dieu nay chung minh muc tieu $3M/lan phong cua Starship la kha thi:
  - $1.1M nhien lieu
  - $1M khau hao phuong tien (qua 100+ lan bay)
  - $0.9M van hanh
  
  Tai su dung phan cung, khong phai nhien lieu, la chia khoa cho phong re.
""")
```

**Cac chi so chinh:**

| Chi so | Cong thuc |
|--------|-----------|
| Chi phi moi kg | Gia phong / Cong suat LEO |
| Lan phong chom sao | ceil(so_vt / (Cong_suat_LEO / khoi_luong_vt)) |
| Tong chi phi phong | Lan phong × gia_moi_lan_phong |
| So lan bay hoa von | Chi phi phuong tien / (gia - chi phi bien) |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **So sanh gia niem yet.** Chi phi noi bo SpaceX la ~$15M/lan bay; gia ben ngoai la $67M. Starlink tra noi bo.

2. **Bo qua rideshare.** Cubesat 100 kg khong can $67M — no tra ~$500k tren Transporter.

3. **Quen vo bao ve tai trong.** Ve tinh nang co the bi gioi han the tich, khong phai khoi luong. $/kg gay hieu lam.

4. **Gia dinh ti le tuyen tinh.** Starship tai $200/kg khong co nghia 100 kg ton $20k — co gia toi thieu.

5. **Bo qua chi phi tich hop.** Phong la 30-50% tong chi phi nhiem vu. He thong mat dat va van hanh cong don.

6. **Ngoai suy Starship qua som.** Starship san xuat con nhieu nam nua. Lap ke hoach theo gia Falcon 9 cho 2024-2026.

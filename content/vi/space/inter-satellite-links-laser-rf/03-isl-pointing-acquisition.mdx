## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Truoc khi hai ve tinh co the trao doi du lieu qua ISL, truoc tien chung phai tim thay nhau. Cho lien ket quang voi do rong buc xa duoi giay cung, dieu nay giong nhu chieu but laser tu New York va ban trung dong xu o Los Angeles—trong khi ca hai dang di chuyen o toc do 7 km/s. He thong Chi huong, Thu, va Theo doi (PAT) thuong la he thong con kho khan nhat cua dau cuoi laser. Hieu trinh tu PAT la can thiet cho thiet ke dau cuoi va lap lich lien ket.

## Ap dung

**Tinh toan dinh co he thong PAT:**

```python
import numpy as np

# Hang so
C = 299792458  # m/s

def point_ahead_angle_urad(relative_velocity_km_s):
    """Tinh goc chi huong truoc."""
    v = relative_velocity_km_s * 1000  # m/s
    return 2 * v / C * 1e6  # μrad

def acquisition_time_spiral(uncertainty_urad, beam_urad, scan_rate_urad_s):
    """Uoc tinh thoi gian thu quet xoan (truong hop xau nhat)."""
    area_ratio = (uncertainty_urad / beam_urad) ** 2
    scan_time = uncertainty_urad / scan_rate_urad_s
    return scan_time * np.sqrt(area_ratio)

def acquisition_time_staring(uncertainty_urad, beacon_urad, snr_required_db):
    """Kiem tra thu nhin co dinh co kha thi khong."""
    coverage = (beacon_urad / uncertainty_urad) ** 2
    power_penalty = -20 * np.log10(beacon_urad / uncertainty_urad)
    return coverage >= 1, power_penalty

def tracking_bandwidth_required(jitter_freq_hz, rejection_db):
    """Bang thong vong can thiet cho loai bo nhieu dong."""
    # Cho loai bo -3dB tai jitter_freq, can BW >> jitter_freq
    # Cho loai bo -20dB, can BW ≈ 10 × jitter_freq
    factor = 10 ** (rejection_db / 20)
    return jitter_freq_hz * factor

def fsm_stroke_required(uncertainty_urad, margin=1.5):
    """Hanh trinh co khi FSM can thiet."""
    # Goc quang = 2 × goc co khi cho phan xa
    mechanical_urad = uncertainty_urad * margin / 2
    return mechanical_urad

# Phan tich
print("Phan tich He thong PAT")
print("=" * 60)

# Chi huong truoc
print("\n1. Yeu cau Goc Chi huong Truoc")
print("-" * 60)
scenarios = [
    ('LEO-LEO trong mat phang', 0.5),
    ('LEO-LEO giua mat phang', 5.0),
    ('LEO-LEO truong hop xau', 15.0),
    ('GEO-LEO', 3.0),
    ('GEO-GEO', 0.1),
]

print(f"  {'Kich ban':25} {'v_rel (km/s)':>12} {'θ_pa (μrad)':>12} {'θ_pa (giay cung)':>16}")
print(f"  {'-'*25} {'-'*12} {'-'*12} {'-'*16}")
for name, v_rel in scenarios:
    pa = point_ahead_angle_urad(v_rel)
    arcsec = pa * 0.206
    print(f"  {name:25} {v_rel:>12.1f} {pa:>12.1f} {arcsec:>16.1f}")

# Thoi gian thu
print("\n2. Phan tich Thoi gian Thu")
print("-" * 60)
uncertainty = 500  # μrad (tu ephemeris + loi tu the)
beam = 50  # μrad (buc xa truyen thong)
beacon = 200  # μrad (beacon thu rong)
scan_rate = 100  # μrad/s

print(f"  Non khong chac chan: {uncertainty} μrad")
print(f"  Buc xa truyen thong: {beam} μrad")
print(f"  Buc xa beacon: {beacon} μrad")

# Quet xoan
t_spiral = acquisition_time_spiral(uncertainty, beam, scan_rate)
print(f"\n  Quet xoan (buc xa hep):")
print(f"    Thoi gian truong hop xau: {t_spiral:.1f} s")

# Nhin co dinh voi beacon
feasible, penalty = acquisition_time_staring(uncertainty, beacon, 10)
print(f"\n  Nhin co dinh (beacon rong):")
print(f"    Kha thi: {feasible}")
print(f"    Hinh phat cong suat: {penalty:.1f} dB")

# Yeu cau theo doi
print("\n3. Yeu cau Vong Theo doi")
print("-" * 60)
disturbances = [
    ('Rung banh xe phan ung', 150, 20),
    ('Dao dong tam pin', 0.5, 30),
    ('Troi nhiet', 0.001, 40),
]

for name, freq, rejection in disturbances:

    print(f"  {name:25} {freq:>10.3f} {rejection:>8} dB {bw:>10.1f} Hz")

    ('Bat theo doi tinh', 0.2, 'T-40.3s'),
    ('Thiet lap lien ket', 0.1, 'T-40.2s'),
    ('Bat dau truyen du lieu', 0, 'T-40s'),
]

total_time = 0
print(f"  {'Giai doan':30} {'Thoi gian':>10} {'Dau thoi gian':>14}")
print(f"  {'-'*30} {'-'*10} {'-'*14}")
for phase, duration, timestamp in sequence:
    print(f"  {phase:30} {duration:>8.1f} s {timestamp:>14}")
    total_time += duration

print(f"\n  Tong thoi gian thu: ~{20} s (trung binh)")
print(f"  Voi thu lai (truong hop 3σ): ~{60} s")
```

## Cam bay

1. **Tinh sai chi huong truoc.** Quen chi huong truoc hoac tinh sai dau gay mat lien ket.

2. **Qua thoi gian thu.** Neu mau quet khong phu vung khong chac chan, lien ket khong bao gio thiet lap.

3. **Danh doi cong suat beacon.** Beacon rong = thu nhanh hon nhung bien do tam it hon.

4. **Vong theo doi khong on dinh.** Bang thong qua cao gay dao dong; qua thap bo lo jitter.

5. **FSM bao hoa.** Neu nhieu dong vuot qua pham vi FSM, theo doi that bai dot ngot.

6. **Mat Troi trong truong nhin.** Cam bien thu bao hoa; phai lap lich tranh goc Mat Troi.

7. **Khoang trong chuyen giao.** Cat mot lien ket de thu lien ket khac mat 10-60s—lap ke hoach dinh tuyen tuong ung.

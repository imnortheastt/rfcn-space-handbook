## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Den duoc quy dao la nua tran chien—o lai do la nua con lai. Ve tinh doi mat voi nhieu loan lien tuc tu bat thuong trong luc, ap suat buc xa mat troi, va luc can khi quyen. Duy tri vi tri tieu thu 10-50 m/s delta-v moi nam, va dieu nay truc tiep xac dinh tuoi tho nhiem vu. Hieu cac luc nay la can thiet de lap ngan sach nhien lieu va quan ly chom sao.

## Ap dung

**Tinh toan duy tri vi tri:**

```python
import numpy as np

def toc_do_troi_dt_geo(kinh_do_deg):
    """
    Uoc tinh gia toc troi D-T do ba truc Trai dat.
    """
    on_dinh_dong = 75
    on_dinh_tay = 255
    
    kc_dong = abs(kinh_do_deg - on_dinh_dong)
    kc_tay = abs(kinh_do_deg - on_dinh_tay)
    
    max_gia_toc = 0.002  # do/ngay²
    
    if kc_dong < kc_tay:
        gia_toc = max_gia_toc * np.sin(np.radians(2 * (kinh_do_deg - on_dinh_dong)))
    else:
        gia_toc = max_gia_toc * np.sin(np.radians(2 * (kinh_do_deg - on_dinh_tay)))
    
    return gia_toc

def delta_v_bn_geo_moi_nam(do_nghieng_dich_deg=0.05):
    """
    Delta-v hang nam cho duy tri vi tri B-N.
    """
    tang_do_nghieng_moi_nam = 0.85  # do
    v_geo = 3.07  # km/s
    
    delta_i_rad = np.radians(tang_do_nghieng_moi_nam)
    delta_v = 2 * v_geo * np.sin(delta_i_rad / 2)
    
    return delta_v * 1000  # m/s

def delta_v_luc_can_leo(do_cao_km, ti_le_dien_tich_khoi_luong, nam=1):
    """
    Uoc tinh delta-v can de bu luc can.
    """
    rho_400 = 3e-12  # kg/m³ tai 400 km
    do_cao_ty_le = 60  # km
    
    rho = rho_400 * np.exp(-(do_cao_km - 400) / do_cao_ty_le)
    
    v = 7.7  # km/s xap xi cho LEO
    
    C_D = 2.2
    a_luc_can = 0.5 * rho * (v * 1000)**2 * C_D * ti_le_dien_tich_khoi_luong
    
    giay_moi_nam = 365.25 * 24 * 3600
    delta_v = a_luc_can * giay_moi_nam * nam
    
    return delta_v

# Tinh toan vi du
print("Phan tich Duy tri Vi tri")
print("=" * 60)

# Troi D-T GEO
print("\n1. Troi Dong-Tay GEO theo Kinh do")

for kd in kinh_do:

    print(f"  {kd:>12} {troi:>15.6f} {dv_nam:>12.1f} {on_dinh}")

# Duy tri B-N GEO
print("\n2. Duy tri Vi tri Bac-Nam GEO")
print("-" * 60)

bn_nam = delta_v_bn_geo_moi_nam()
print(f"  Toc do tang do nghieng: ~0.85°/nam")
print(f"  Δv B-N nam can thiet: {bn_nam:.1f} m/s")
print(f"")
print(f"  Ngan sach B-N nhiem vu 15 nam: {bn_nam * 15:.0f} m/s")
print(f"  Day thuong la 90% ngan sach duy tri vi tri!")

# Tong duy tri vi tri GEO

print("-" * 60)

print("\n4. Luc can LEO va Yeu cau Tang toc")
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Danh gia thap chi phi B-N.** Dieu khien B-N chiem uu the ngan sach GEO; D-T gan nhu mien phi so sanh.

2. **Bo qua chu ky mat troi.** Cuc dai mat troi co the tang 10× luc can LEO; lap ngan sach cho truong hop xau nhat.

3. **Chu ky hoat dong day dien.** Dong co EP chay nhieu gio moi ngay—len ke hoach dien va nhiet phu hop.

4. **Tiet kiem GEO nghieng.** Neu anten mat dat co the theo doi, bo dieu khien B-N va tiet kiem 90% ngan sach SK.

5. **Tranh va cham.** Thao tac tranh manh rac lay tu cung ngan sach nhien lieu.

6. **Du tru cuoi doi.** Phai de danh nhien lieu cho quy dao phoc hoi hoac xuong quy dao—dung het ngan sach duy tri vi tri.

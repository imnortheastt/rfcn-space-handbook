## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Co hang ty thiet bi IoT can ket noi ngoai vung phu song di dong: container van chuyen giua dai duong, may theo doi gia suc o trang trai xa xoi, cam bien duong ong trong sa mac. NR-NTN nham vao smartphone, nhung IoT-NTN nham vao phan duoi dai — thiet bi truyen kilobyte moi ngay, ngu hang gio, va chay bang mot pin trong mot thap ky. Release 17 dieu chinh NB-IoT va LTE-M cho ve tinh, cho phep phu song IoT toan cau thuc su voi thay doi toi thieu cho chipset hien co.

## Ap dung

**May tinh ngan sach lien ket va tuoi tho pin IoT-NTN:**

```python
import math

def ngan_sach_lien_ket_iot_ntn(tan_so_ghz, do_cao_ve_tinh_km, goc_nang_do,
                                cong_suat_ue_dbm, anten_ue_dbi, ve_tinh_gt_db,
                                bang_thong_khz, so_lap_lai):
    """
    Tinh ngan sach lien ket IoT-NTN voi tang ich lap lai.
    """
    ban_kinh_trai_dat = 6371
    el_rad = math.radians(goc_nang_do)
    
    khoang_cach_nghieng = (-ban_kinh_trai_dat * math.sin(el_rad) + 
                           math.sqrt((ban_kinh_trai_dat * math.sin(el_rad))**2 + 
                                    2 * ban_kinh_trai_dat * do_cao_ve_tinh_km + do_cao_ve_tinh_km**2))
    
    fspl = 32.45 + 20 * math.log10(tan_so_ghz * 1000) + 20 * math.log10(khoang_cach_nghieng)
    
    ue_eirp = cong_suat_ue_dbm + anten_ue_dbi
    
    tang_ich_lap_lai = 10 * math.log10(so_lap_lai)
    
    cong_suat_nhan = ue_eirp - fspl + ve_tinh_gt_db + tang_ich_lap_lai
    
    mcl = fspl - anten_ue_dbi - ve_tinh_gt_db - tang_ich_lap_lai
    
    return {
        "khoang_cach_nghieng_km": khoang_cach_nghieng,
        "fspl_db": fspl,
        "tang_ich_lap_lai_db": tang_ich_lap_lai,
        "mcl_db": mcl
    }

def uoc_tinh_tuoi_tho_pin(dung_luong_pin_mah, tx_ma, rx_ma, ngu_ua,
                           thoi_gian_tx_s_moi_ngay, thoi_gian_rx_s_moi_ngay):
    """
    Uoc tinh tuoi tho pin thiet bi IoT.
    """
    tx_gio_moi_ngay = thoi_gian_tx_s_moi_ngay / 3600
    rx_gio_moi_ngay = thoi_gian_rx_s_moi_ngay / 3600
    ngu_gio_moi_ngay = 24 - tx_gio_moi_ngay - rx_gio_moi_ngay
    
    tieu_thu_hang_ngay_mah = (
        tx_ma * tx_gio_moi_ngay +
        rx_ma * rx_gio_moi_ngay +
        (ngu_ua / 1000) * ngu_gio_moi_ngay
    )
    
    pin_ngay = dung_luong_pin_mah / tieu_thu_hang_ngay_mah
    pin_nam = pin_ngay / 365
    
    return {
        "tieu_thu_hang_ngay_mah": tieu_thu_hang_ngay_mah,
        "pin_ngay": pin_ngay,
        "pin_nam": pin_nam
    }
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Bo qua cong suat GNSS.** Thu GNSS tieu thu 20-50 mA trong 30+ giay. Tinh vao trong tinh toan pin.

2. **Quen kha nang nhin thay ve tinh.** Thiet bi IoT khong the truyen khi khong co ve tinh tren dau. Lap lich truyen cho cua so nhin thay.

3. **Toi uu qua muc cho thong luong.** IoT-NTN la ve phu song, khong phai toc do. 100 byte/gio la du cho hau het cam bien.

4. **Danh gia thap tac dong lap lai.** 2048× lap lai nghia la tin nhan 100 byte mat ~10 giay thoi gian phat. Lap ke hoach phu hop.

5. **Tron lan gia dinh mat dat va NTN.** NB-IoT mat dat co MCL 144 dB. NTN can 170+ dB. Thiet ke cho truong hop kho hon.

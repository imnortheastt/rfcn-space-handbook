## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

"Tai sao dien thoai cua toi khong the ket noi truc tiep voi Starlink?" la cau hoi moi ky su deu nghe. Cau tra loi lien quan den vat ly ma khong luong phan mem nao co the vuot qua: smartphone co anten nho, cong suat phat han che, va khong the huong len troi. Ket noi chung voi ve tinh 600 km doi hoi dong ngan sach lien ket xau hon 20+ dB so voi di dong mat dat. Bai hoc nay giai thich tai sao direct-to-device ve tinh ve co ban la kho, va cach cac dich vu nhu T-Mobile/Starlink va AST SpaceMobile tiep can van de khac nhau.

## Ap dung

**May tinh ngan sach lien ket direct-to-device:**

```python
import math

def ngan_sach_ul_d2d(tan_so_ghz, do_cao_ve_tinh_km, goc_nang_do,
                      cong_suat_ue_dbm, anten_ue_dbi,
                      dien_tich_anten_ve_tinh_m2, nhiet_do_nhieu_ve_tinh_k,
                      bang_thong_hz, so_lap_lai):
    """
    Tinh ngan sach duong len direct-to-device.
    """
    ban_kinh_trai_dat = 6371
    el_rad = math.radians(goc_nang_do)
    
    khoang_cach_nghieng = (-ban_kinh_trai_dat * math.sin(el_rad) + 
                           math.sqrt((ban_kinh_trai_dat * math.sin(el_rad))**2 + 
                                    2 * ban_kinh_trai_dat * do_cao_ve_tinh_km + do_cao_ve_tinh_km**2))
    
    c = 3e8
    buoc_song = c / (tan_so_ghz * 1e9)
    
    fspl = 20 * math.log10(4 * math.pi * khoang_cach_nghieng * 1000 / buoc_song)
    
    ae = dien_tich_anten_ve_tinh_m2 * 0.6
    tang_ich_anten_ve_tinh = 10 * math.log10(4 * math.pi * ae / buoc_song**2)
    
    suy_hao_co_the = 4
    suy_hao_phan_cuc = 3
    suy_hao_khi_quyen = 0.5
    tong_suy_hao = fspl + suy_hao_co_the + suy_hao_phan_cuc + suy_hao_khi_quyen
    
    tang_ich_lap_lai = 10 * math.log10(so_lap_lai)
    
    return {
        "khoang_cach_nghieng_km": khoang_cach_nghieng,
        "fspl_db": fspl,
        "tang_ich_anten_ve_tinh_dbi": tang_ich_anten_ve_tinh,
        "tong_suy_hao_db": tong_suy_hao,
        "tang_ich_lap_lai_db": tang_ich_lap_lai
    }
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Gia dinh DL = UL.** Ngan sach duong xuong tot hon 30+ dB. Dich vu bi gioi han boi UL, khong phai DL.

2. **Bo qua suy hao co the.** Dien thoai giu ap vao co the mat 3-6 dB. Thuc te xau hon dac ta.

3. **Quen ve trong nha.** Ve tinh D2D yeu cau tam nhin thang. Khong co tin hieu qua toa nha.

4. **Hua qua muc ve thong luong.** "5G tu khong gian" nghe hay. Thuc te: tin nhan van ban cho hau het nguoi dung.

5. **Bo qua thuc te pho.** D2D can doi tac MNO cho pho. Nha khai thac ve tinh thuan tuy khong the lam mot minh.

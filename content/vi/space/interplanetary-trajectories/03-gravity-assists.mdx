## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Voyager 1 va 2 da tham bon hanh tinh voi ngan sach nhien lieu le ra chi du den mot. Cassini su dung bon lan ho tro hap dan de den Sao Tho. New Horizons bay ngang qua Sao Moc de cat thoi gian den Sao Diemvuong nhieu nam. Ho tro hap dan la cach duy nhat de den cac dich xa voi ngan sach khoi luong hop ly—va chung mien phi.

## Ap dung

**Tinh toan ho tro hap dan:**

```python
import numpy as np

def turn_angle(v_inf, r_periapsis, mu_planet):
    """
    Tinh goc quay hyperbolic.
    
    v_inf: van toc du hyperbolic (km/s)
    r_periapsis: khoang cach tiep can gan nhat (km)
    mu_planet: tham so hap dan (km³/s²)
    
    Tra ve: goc quay tinh bang do
    """
    # Do lech tam
    e = 1 + r_periapsis * v_inf**2 / mu_planet
    
    # Goc quay
    delta_rad = 2 * np.arcsin(1 / e)
    return np.degrees(delta_rad)

def gravity_assist_dv(v_inf, turn_angle_deg):
    """
    Tinh do lon thay doi van toc nhat tam.
    
    Tra ve: |ΔV| tinh bang km/s
    """
    delta_rad = np.radians(turn_angle_deg)
    return 2 * v_inf * np.sin(delta_rad / 2)

def max_possible_turn(v_inf, r_min, mu_planet):
    """
    Goc quay toi da cho can diem toi thieu.
    """
    return turn_angle(v_inf, r_min, mu_planet)

# Tham so hanh tinh (km³/s², km, km/s)
PLANETS = {
    'Sao Kim': {'mu': 324859, 'r_min': 6151, 'v_orbit': 35.0},
    'Trai Dat': {'mu': 398600, 'r_min': 6578, 'v_orbit': 29.8},
    'Sao Hoa':  {'mu': 42828,  'r_min': 3590, 'v_orbit': 24.1},
    'Sao Moc': {'mu': 126686534, 'r_min': 75000, 'v_orbit': 13.1},
    'Sao Tho': {'mu': 37931187, 'r_min': 65000, 'v_orbit': 9.7},
}

print("Phan tich Ho tro Hap dan:")
print("=" * 60)

# Vi du: Voyager tai Sao Moc
v_inf_jupiter = 10.0  # km/s (dien hinh cho bay ngang Sao Moc)
r_p_jupiter = 350000  # km (Voyager 1 ~349,000 km)

delta = turn_angle(v_inf_jupiter, r_p_jupiter, PLANETS['Sao Moc']['mu'])
dv = gravity_assist_dv(v_inf_jupiter, delta)

print(f"\nBay ngang Sao Moc giong Voyager:")
print(f"  V_vo cuc: {v_inf_jupiter} km/s")
print(f"  Can diem: {r_p_jupiter:,} km (~5 ban kinh Sao Moc)")
print(f"  Goc quay: {delta:.1f}°")
print(f"  ΔV nhat tam: {dv:.1f} km/s")

# So sanh tat ca hanh tinh tai cung V_inf
print(f"\nΔV toi da tai V∞ = 8 km/s:")
print("-" * 60)
v_inf_test = 8.0

for name, p in PLANETS.items():
    delta = max_possible_turn(v_inf_test, p['r_min'], p['mu'])
    dv = gravity_assist_dv(v_inf_test, delta)
    print(f"  {name:10} Quay: {delta:5.1f}°  ΔV: {dv:5.1f} km/s")

# Vi du nhieu bay ngang
print(f"\nQuy dao VVEJGA kieu Cassini:")
print("  Bay ngang Sao Kim 1: ~2 km/s tang")
print("  Bay ngang Sao Kim 2: ~2 km/s tang")  
print("  Bay ngang Trai Dat:  ~4 km/s tang")
print("  Bay ngang Sao Moc:   ~3 km/s tang")
print("  Tong ΔV mien phi: ~11 km/s (vs ~3 km/s ngan sach NL)")
```

**Cac nhiem vu ho tro hap dan noi tieng:**

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Thoi gian la tat ca.** Hanh tinh phai o vi tri dung; co hoi hiem.

2. **Phat doi mat phang.** Thay doi do nghieng trong bay ngang ton "ngan sach goc quay."

3. **Khong luon nhanh hon.** Duong vong voi ho tro co the mat nhieu thoi gian hon truc tiep.

4. **Rang buoc V∞.** Chi co the quay nhung gi ban mang; can nang luong ban dau de den hanh tinh.

5. **Ngam quan trong.** Sai so mat phang B nho tro thanh sai so quy dao lon.

6. **Nguy hiem buc xa.** Bay ngang Sao Moc phoi tau voi buc xa du doi.

7. **Tuong tac ve tinh.** Cac ve tinh lon co the nhieu loan quy dao bay ngang.

<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

"Ve tinh nay dang bay nhanh bao nhieu luc nay?" Phuong trinh vis-viva tra loi chi trong mot buoc — khong can moment dong luong goc, khong can tinh thoi gian bay, chi can vi tri hien tai va kich thuoc quy dao.

Moi tinh toan delta-v ban se thuc hien — chuyen quy dao Hohmann, chen vao GTO, hay khoi hanh lien hanh tinh — deu bat dau tu day. Starlink, GPS, ten lua Ariane 5, cac tau tham do Voyager — tat ca deu duoc thiet ke voi vis-viva lam nen tang. Day la cong thuc huu ich nhat trong co hoc quy dao.

</section>

<section class="lesson-intuition">

## Truc giac

### Phuong trinh

$$v^2 = \mu \left(\frac{2}{r} - \frac{1}{a}\right)$$

Trong do:
- $v$ = toc do quy dao tai vi tri hien tai [km/s]
- $\mu$ = tham so trong truong = $GM$ [km³/s²]
- $r$ = khoang cach hien tai tu tam vat the trung tam [km]
- $a$ = ban truc lon cua quy dao [km]

Voi Trai Dat: $\mu = 398600.4418$ km³/s²

### Goc do nang luong

Phuong trinh vis-viva chinh la bao toan nang luong duoc viet lai.

Nang luong co hoc rieng $\varepsilon$ (nang luong tren don vi khoi luong) co hai thanh phan:

$$\varepsilon = \underbrace{\frac{v^2}{2}}_{\text{dong nang}} - \underbrace{\frac{\mu}{r}}_{\text{the nang}} = -\frac{\mu}{2a} = \text{hang so}$$

Tong nang luong duoc xac dinh boi ban truc lon $a$ ma thoi. Bien doi lai:

$$\frac{v^2}{2} = \frac{\mu}{r} - \frac{\mu}{2a} = \mu\left(\frac{1}{r} - \frac{1}{2a}\right)$$

Nhan doi hai ve la co phuong trinh vis-viva.

```
Su doi thanh nang luong doc theo quy dao ellipse:

     Apoapsis (cham)                Periapsis (nhanh)
          o                               *
     Dong nang thap, the nang cao   Dong nang cao, the nang thap
     v_a ~ 1.6 km/s                 v_p ~ 10.2 km/s
     (vi du GTO)                    (vi du GTO)

     Tong epsilon = -mu/2a = hang so xuyen suot
```

### Loai quy dao xac dinh boi dau nang luong

| Nang luong $\varepsilon$ | Ban truc lon $a$ | Loai quy dao |
|---|---|---|
| $\varepsilon < 0$ | $a > 0$ | Ellipse (bi rang buoc) |
| $\varepsilon = 0$ | $a \to \infty$ | Parabol (thoat ly bien) |
| $\varepsilon > 0$ | $a < 0$ | Hyperbol (quy dao thoat ly) |

### Su thay doi toc do doc theo ellipse

Tai diem chua (periapsis, $r = r_p = a(1-e)$) va diem vien (apoapsis, $r = r_a = a(1+e)$), van toc hoan toan theo phuong tiep tuyen:

$$v_p = \sqrt{\frac{\mu}{a} \cdot \frac{1+e}{1-e}} \qquad v_a = \sqrt{\frac{\mu}{a} \cdot \frac{1-e}{1+e}}$$

Ti le van toc:

$$\frac{v_p}{v_a} = \frac{1+e}{1-e} = \frac{r_a}{r_p}$$

Voi GTO ($r_p = 6678$ km, $r_a = 42164$ km): ti le = 6.3 — diem chua nhanh hon diem vien hon 6 lan.

</section>

<section class="lesson-mechanism">

## Co che hoat dong

### Suy dien tu bao toan nang luong

Bat dau voi nang luong co hoc rieng, hang so xuyen suot quy dao:

$$\varepsilon = \frac{v^2}{2} - \frac{\mu}{r} = \text{hang so}$$

Tinh tai periapsis noi $r = a(1-e)$ va $v = v_p$. Su dung bao toan moment dong luong goc ($h = r_p v_p$ tai periapsis):

$$h = \sqrt{\mu a (1-e^2)}$$

$$v_p = \frac{h}{r_p} = \sqrt{\frac{\mu(1+e)}{a(1-e)}}$$

Do do:

$$\varepsilon = \frac{v_p^2}{2} - \frac{\mu}{r_p} = -\frac{\mu}{2a}$$

Vi $\varepsilon$ la hang so, tai bat ky diem nao khac:

$$\frac{v^2}{2} - \frac{\mu}{r} = -\frac{\mu}{2a}$$

$$\boxed{v^2 = \mu\left(\frac{2}{r} - \frac{1}{a}\right)}$$

### Cac truong hop dac biet

**Quy dao tron** ($r = a$ moi noi):

$$v_c = \sqrt{\frac{\mu}{r}}$$

ISS tai $r = 6778$ km: $v_c = \sqrt{398600/6778} = 7.667$ km/s

**Van toc thoat ly** (parabol, $a \to \infty$, $\varepsilon = 0$):

$$v_{thoat} = \sqrt{\frac{2\mu}{r}} = \sqrt{2} \cdot v_c$$

Tu be mat Trai Dat: $v_{thoat} = \sqrt{2 \times 398600 / 6378} = 11.18$ km/s

**Van toc du thua cua hyperbol** ($v_\infty$):

Khi $r \to \infty$ tren quy dao hyperbol: $v^2 \to -\mu/a = v_\infty^2$.

Tai bat ky ban kinh: $v^2 = v_{thoat}^2 + v_\infty^2$

Day la chi so C3 trong thiet ke nhiem vu lien hanh tinh ($C_3 = v_\infty^2$ tinh bang km²/s²).

### Tinh toan delta-v qua vis-viva

Moi co dong quy dao la mot delta-v: su thay doi van toc giua hai quy dao tai cung mot diem.

**Vi du chuyen quy dao Hohmann — LEO len GEO:**

Ban truc lon quy dao chuyen tiep: $a_t = (r_{LEO} + r_{GEO})/2 = (6778 + 42164)/2 = 24471$ km

Dot chay 1 tai $r = r_{LEO} = 6778$ km:
- Tron hien tai: $v_1 = \sqrt{398600/6778} = 7.667$ km/s
- Quy dao chuyen tiep tai periapsis: $v_{t,p} = \sqrt{398600(2/6778 - 1/24471)} = 10.10$ km/s
- $\Delta v_1 = 10.10 - 7.667 = 2.43$ km/s

Dot chay 2 tai $r = r_{GEO} = 42164$ km:
- Quy dao chuyen tiep tai apoapsis: $v_{t,a} = \sqrt{398600(2/42164 - 1/24471)} = 1.63$ km/s
- GEO tron: $v_2 = \sqrt{398600/42164} = 3.07$ km/s
- $\Delta v_2 = 3.07 - 1.63 = 1.44$ km/s

Tong $\Delta v = 2.43 + 1.44 = 3.87$ km/s de di tu LEO len GEO.

```
Ngan sach Delta-v: LEO → GEO Hohmann

  r_LEO = 6.778 km   v = 7.667 km/s   → Dv1 = +2.43 km/s
                            |
                    Quy dao chuyen tiep
                    a = 24.471 km
                            |
  r_GEO = 42.164 km  v = 1.63 km/s   → Dv2 = +1.44 km/s
                            |
                    GEO v = 3.07 km/s

  Tong Dv = 3.87 km/s
```

### Nang luong va kich thuoc quy dao

Hai quy dao co cung ban truc lon thi co nang luong va chu ky giong het nhau — bat ke do lech tam. Quy dao tron tai $a = 42164$ km va quy dao ellipse rat bet voi $r_p = 1000$ km, $r_a = 83328$ km deu co nang luong va chu ky 24 gio nhu nhau.

Dieu nay co mot he qua thuc tien: de nang/ha quy dao, ban phai thay doi nang luong, tuc la thay doi $a$. Nguoc lai, thay doi mat phang quay vecto van toc ma khong thay doi toc do (neu thuc hien tai diem thich hop), nen khong thay doi nang luong hay $a$.

## Ap dung

**Bo cong cu vis-viva day du:**

```python
import numpy as np

MU_EARTH = 398600.4418  # km^3/s^2
R_EARTH = 6378.137      # km

def vis_viva(r, a, mu=MU_EARTH):
    """
    Toc do quy dao tai ban kinh r voi ban truc lon a.
    r, a tinh bang km; tra ve km/s.
    a < 0 voi hyperbol.
    """
    return np.sqrt(mu * (2/r - 1/a))

def circular_velocity(r, mu=MU_EARTH):
    return np.sqrt(mu / r)

def escape_velocity(r, mu=MU_EARTH):
    return np.sqrt(2 * mu / r)

def specific_energy(a, mu=MU_EARTH):
    """Nang luong co hoc rieng tinh bang km^2/s^2."""
    return -mu / (2 * a)

def periapsis_velocity(a, e, mu=MU_EARTH):
    return np.sqrt(mu / a * (1 + e) / (1 - e))

def apoapsis_velocity(a, e, mu=MU_EARTH):
    return np.sqrt(mu / a * (1 - e) / (1 + e))

def hohmann_dv(r1, r2, mu=MU_EARTH):
    """
    Tong delta-v cho chuyen quy dao Hohmann giua hai quy dao tron.
    Tra ve (dv1, dv2, dv_total) tinh bang km/s.
    """
    a_t = (r1 + r2) / 2
    v1_circ = circular_velocity(r1, mu)
    v_tp    = vis_viva(r1, a_t, mu)
    dv1 = v_tp - v1_circ
    v_ta    = vis_viva(r2, a_t, mu)
    v2_circ = circular_velocity(r2, mu)
    dv2 = v2_circ - v_ta
    return dv1, dv2, dv1 + dv2

# --- Cac quy dao pho bien ---
print("=== Van toc quy dao ===")
orbits = [
    ("LEO (ISS, 400 km)",   R_EARTH + 400),
    ("MEO (GPS, 20180 km)", R_EARTH + 20180),
    ("GEO (35786 km)",      R_EARTH + 35786),
]
for name, r in orbits:
    vc  = circular_velocity(r)
    ve  = escape_velocity(r)
    eps = specific_energy(r)
    print(f"{name}")
    print(f"  v_tron = {vc:.3f} km/s  v_thoat = {ve:.3f} km/s  eps = {eps:.1f} km^2/s^2")

# --- Vi du GTO ---
r_p = R_EARTH + 300
r_a = R_EARTH + 35786
a   = (r_p + r_a) / 2
e   = (r_a - r_p) / (r_a + r_p)
print(f"\n=== GTO (300 km x GEO) ===")
print(f"  a = {a:.0f} km,  e = {e:.4f}")
print(f"  v_periapsis = {vis_viva(r_p, a):.3f} km/s")
print(f"  v_apoapsis  = {vis_viva(r_a, a):.3f} km/s")

# --- Delta-v Hohmann ---
dv1, dv2, total = hohmann_dv(R_EARTH + 400, R_EARTH + 35786)
print(f"\n=== LEO (400 km) → GEO Hohmann ===")
print(f"  Dv1 = {dv1:.3f} km/s  (LEO → periapsis GTO)")
print(f"  Dv2 = {dv2:.3f} km/s  (apoapsis GTO → GEO)")
print(f"  Tong Dv = {total:.3f} km/s")
```

**Bang tham chieu — van toc quy dao pho bien:**

| Quy dao | Do cao | $r$ [km] | $v_c$ [km/s] | $v_{thoat}$ [km/s] | $\varepsilon$ [km²/s²] |
|---------|--------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|------------------------|
| LEO (ISS) | 400 km | 6.778 | 7.667 | 10.843 | −29.4 |
| Sun-sync | 700 km | 7.078 | 7.504 | 10.613 | −28.2 |
| MEO (GPS) | 20.180 km | 26.558 | 3.874 | 5.479 | −7.5 |
| GEO | 35.786 km | 42.164 | 3.075 | 4.349 | −4.7 |
| Be mat Trai Dat | 0 km | 6.378 | 7.905 | 11.180 | −31.2 |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Nhung bay an

1. **Dau cua $a$ voi hyperbol.** Quy dao hyperbol co $a < 0$, nen $-1/a > 0$. Vis-viva van cho $v$ duong that — khong lay gia tri tuyet doi cua $a$ truoc khi tinh.

2. **Ban kinh vs do cao.** $r$ duoc do tu tam Trai Dat: $r = R_E + h$. Dung do cao $h$ truc tiep se cho van toc sai khoang 15%.

3. **Thoat ly khong co nghia la "v=0 ngay bay gio".** Van toc thoat ly co nghia la vat the dat den $r \to \infty$ voi $v \to 0$. Tai diem phong, ban van can $v_{thoat}$ — day la van toc toi thieu de roi khoi gieng trong truong tu ban kinh do.

4. **$v_{thoat} = \sqrt{2} \cdot v_c$.** Ti le nay dung chinh xac tai moi ban kinh. Huu ich de kiem tra nhanh.

5. **Don vi.** $\mu_{Earth} = 398600.4418$ km³/s² voi $r, a$ tinh bang km cho $v$ tinh bang km/s. Tron lan SI (m, m³/s²) voi km se cho ket qua sai ma khong co canh bao.

6. **Vis-viva gia dinh chuyen dong Keplerian (khong co luc day).** Trong khi dot chay, ve tinh khong o tren quy dao Keplerian. Tinh van toc vis-viva o dau va cuoi moi dot chay, coi moi dot la delta-v xung tuc thoi tai mot diem.

7. **Cung $a$ khong co nghia la cung quy dao.** Hai quy dao voi ban truc lon giong het nhau co nang luong va chu ky nhu nhau nhung khac do lech tam, do nghieng va hinh hoc. Vis-viva cho ban toc do, khong phai huong.

8. **Delta-v tu vis-viva la hieu so vecto.** Voi dot chay Hohmann tai periapsis hay apoapsis, cac vecto van toc song song nen $\Delta v = |v_{moi} - v_{cu}|$. Voi cac dot chay tong quat tai cac diem khac, cac vecto khong song song va ban phai dung dinh ly cosin: $\Delta v = \sqrt{v_1^2 + v_2^2 - 2v_1 v_2 \cos\theta}$.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Thiet bi dau cuoi Starlink "Dishy" co the theo doi nhieu ve tinh LEO lao qua dau voi toc do 7.5 km/s—ma khong co bo phan chuyen dong nhin thay. Phep mau nay la cong nghe mang pha: hang tram hoac hang ngan phan tu anten nho dieu huong chum bang dien tu bang cach dieu chinh pha. Hieu mang pha la can thiet de thiet ke cac thiet bi dau cuoi ve tinh hien dai, tu panel phang tieu dung den anten lai dien tu (ESA) cap doanh nghiep.

## Ap dung

**May tinh phan tich mang pha:**

```python
import numpy as np

C = 299792458  # Toc do anh sang (m/s)

def wavelength_m(frequency_ghz):
    """Tinh buoc song tu tan so."""
    return C / (frequency_ghz * 1e9)

def array_gain_db(n_elements, element_gain_dbi=5, efficiency=0.7):
    """Tinh tang ich mang pha."""
    return 10 * np.log10(n_elements) + element_gain_dbi + 10 * np.log10(efficiency)

def scan_loss_db(theta_deg):
    """Tinh ton hao tang ich do goc quet."""
    theta_rad = np.radians(theta_deg)
    return 10 * np.log10(np.cos(theta_rad))

def max_element_spacing_m(frequency_ghz, max_scan_deg):
    """Tinh khoang cach phan tu toi da de tranh grating lobes."""
    lambda_m = wavelength_m(frequency_ghz)
    theta_rad = np.radians(max_scan_deg)
    return lambda_m / (1 + np.sin(theta_rad))

def phase_shift_deg(element_index, element_spacing_m, frequency_ghz, scan_angle_deg):
    """Tinh dich pha can thiet cho phan tu n."""
    lambda_m = wavelength_m(frequency_ghz)
    theta_rad = np.radians(scan_angle_deg)
    k = 2 * np.pi / lambda_m
    phase_rad = element_index * k * element_spacing_m * np.sin(theta_rad)
    return np.degrees(phase_rad) % 360

# Vi du: Phan tich thiet bi kieu Starlink
print("Phan tich Thiet bi Dau cuoi Mang Pha")
print("=" * 70)

# Thong so
freq_ghz = 12.0  # Downlink Ku-band
n_elements = 1280
element_gain = 5  # dBi
max_scan = 60  # do

print(f"\nThong so He thong:")
print(f"  Tan so: {freq_ghz} GHz")
print(f"  Buoc song: {wavelength_m(freq_ghz)*1000:.2f} mm")
print(f"  So phan tu: {n_elements}")
print(f"  Goc quet toi da: ±{max_scan}°")

# Tang ich mang
print(f"\n1. Phan tich Tang ich Mang")
print("-" * 70)
for n in [64, 256, 1024, 1280, 4096]:
    gain = array_gain_db(n, element_gain)
    print(f"  {n:5} phan tu → {gain:.1f} dBi")

# Ton hao quet
print(f"\n2. Ton hao Quet theo Goc")
print("-" * 70)
print(f"  {'Goc quet':>12} {'Cos(θ)':>10} {'Ton hao':>12} {'Tang ich':>12}")
for theta in [0, 15, 30, 45, 60, 70]:
    loss = scan_loss_db(theta)
    net_gain = array_gain_db(n_elements, element_gain) + loss
    print(f"  {theta:>10}° {np.cos(np.radians(theta)):>10.3f} {loss:>10.1f} dB {net_gain:>10.1f} dBi")

# Khoang cach phan tu
print(f"\n3. Rang buoc Khoang cach Phan tu")
print("-" * 70)
for f in [12.0, 20.0, 30.0]:
    max_d = max_element_spacing_m(f, max_scan) * 1000
    print(f"  {f:.0f} GHz, quet ±60° → d_max = {max_d:.2f} mm")
```

**So sanh thiet bi dau cuoi:**

| Thiet bi | Phan tu | Tang ich | Quet | Bang | Cong suat |
|----------|---------|----------|------|------|-----------|
| Starlink Gen2 | ~1,280 | ~33 dBi | ±60° | Ku/Ka | 50-75W |
| OneWeb | ~256 | ~28 dBi | ±55° | Ku | 30W |
| ESA hang hai | ~1,000+ | ~32 dBi | ±60° | Ka | 100W+ |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Bo qua ton hao quet.** Tai 60° tu thien dinh, ban mat 3 dB—tinh vao ngan sach lien ket.

2. **Grating lobes.** Khoang cach phan tu > λ/2 tai goc quet rong tao chum khong mong muon.

3. **Gioi han nhiet.** ESA cong suat cao sinh nhiet dang ke; thiet ke nhiet la quan trong.

4. **Gia dinh chi phi.** ESA tieu dung mat 20+ nam de tro nen phai chang; doanh nghiep van dat.

5. **Phan cuc.** Mang pha co the lam ca hai phan cuc tron nhung can hieu chuan dung.

6. **Ghep tuong ho.** Cac phan tu anh huong lan nhau; hieu suat thuc khac tinh toan ly tuong.

7. **Gioi han quy dinh.** Mat na EIRP gioi han phat xa ngoai truc de bao ve ve tinh ke can.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Truoc mang pha, da co dia dong co—va chung van o khap noi. Tram mat dat ve tinh GEO, kinh vien vong vo tuyen, he thong radar, va nhieu thiet bi dau cuoi hang hai/hang khong su dung theo doi co khi. ## Ap dung

**Tinh toan theo doi co khi:**

```python
import numpy as np

def van_toc_goc_ve_tinh(do_cao_km, goc_nang_deg):
    """
    Uoc tinh van toc goc cua ve tinh LEO nhin tu mat dat.
    """
    R_trai_dat = 6371  # km
    van_toc_quy_dao = 7.5  # km/s dien hinh cho LEO
    
    el_rad = np.radians(goc_nang_deg)
    khoang_cach_xieng = np.sqrt((R_trai_dat + do_cao_km)**2 - 
                                 (R_trai_dat * np.cos(el_rad))**2) - R_trai_dat * np.sin(el_rad)
    
    van_toc_goc = np.degrees(van_toc_quy_dao / (khoang_cach_xieng + do_cao_km))
    
    return van_toc_goc

def sai_chi_huong_thanh_ton_hao_db(sai_so_deg, do_rong_chum_deg):
    """
    Tinh ton hao tang ich do sai chi huong.
    """
    x = sai_so_deg / do_rong_chum_deg
    ton_hao_db = -12 * x**2
    return ton_hao_db

def tinh_kich_thuoc_dong_co(duong_kinh_dia_m, van_toc_max_deg_s, gia_toc_max_deg_s2):
    """
    Uoc tinh yeu cau mo-men dong co.
    """
    dien_tich = np.pi * (duong_kinh_dia_m/2)**2
    khoi_luong = dien_tich * 10  # kg
    ban_kinh = duong_kinh_dia_m / 2
    quan_tinh = 0.25 * khoi_luong * ban_kinh**2
    
    alpha_rad = np.radians(gia_toc_max_deg_s2)
    mo_men = quan_tinh * alpha_rad
    
    return {
        "khoi_luong_kg": khoi_luong,
        "quan_tinh_kg_m2": quan_tinh,
        "mo_men_nm": mo_men,
    }

# Tinh toan vi du
print("Phan tich Theo doi Co khi")
print("=" * 60)

# Yeu cau theo doi LEO
print("\n1. Van toc Goc LEO theo Goc nang")
print("-" * 60)
do_cao = 550  # km (Starlink)

print(f"  Do cao: {do_cao} km")
print(f"  {'Goc nang':>12} {'Van toc goc':>18} {'Yeu cau':>20}")
for el in [5, 15, 30, 45, 60, 75, 90]:
    van_toc_goc = van_toc_goc_ve_tinh(do_cao, el)
    print(f"  {el:>10}° {van_toc_goc:>16.2f}°/s {'⚠️ Nhanh!' if van_toc_goc > 2 else ''}")

# Anh huong sai chi huong
print("\n2. Anh huong Sai Chi huong len Lien ket")
print("-" * 60)
do_rong_chum = 1.0  # do

print(f"  Do rong chum: {do_rong_chum}°")
print(f"  {'Sai chi huong':>15} {'Ton hao':>12} {'Danh gia':>20}")
for sai_so in [0.0, 0.05, 0.1, 0.2, 0.3, 0.5, 1.0]:
    ton_hao = sai_chi_huong_thanh_ton_hao_db(sai_so, do_rong_chum)
    danh_gia = "OK" if ton_hao > -1 else ("Bien" if ton_hao > -3 else "Nghiem trong")
    print(f"  {sai_so:>13.2f}° {ton_hao:>10.2f} dB {danh_gia:>20}")

# Kich thuoc dong co
print("\n3. Kich thuoc Dong co cho Cac Kich co Dia Khac nhau")
print("-" * 60)
van_toc_max = 10  # deg/s
gia_toc_max = 10  # deg/s²

print(f"  Van toc max: {van_toc_max}°/s, Gia toc max: {gia_toc_max}°/s²")
print(f"  {'Duong kinh':>10} {'Khoi luong':>12} {'Quan tinh':>12} {'Mo-men':>12}")
print(f"  {'(m)':>10} {'(kg)':>12} {'(kg⋅m²)':>12} {'(N⋅m)':>12}")

for duong_kinh in [0.6, 1.2, 2.4, 3.7, 5.0]:
    kich_thuoc = tinh_kich_thuoc_dong_co(duong_kinh, van_toc_max, gia_toc_max)
    print(f"  {duong_kinh:>10.1f} {kich_thuoc['khoi_luong_kg']:>12.0f} {kich_thuoc['quan_tinh_kg_m2']:>12.1f} {kich_thuoc['mo_men_nm']:>12.1f}")

# So sanh GEO vs LEO
print("\n4. So sanh Yeu cau Theo doi GEO vs LEO")
print("-" * 60)
so_sanh = [
    ("Do cao quy dao", "36,000 km", "400-2000 km"),
    ("Van toc goc", "<0.01°/ngay", "0.5-3°/s"),
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Lo khoa tai thien dinh.** Gia do Az-El that bai cho lan qua qua dau; can X-Y hoac chap nhan mat tin hieu.

2. **Tai gio.** Dia lon can vi tri cat va gioi han toc do gio; theo doi suy giam trong gio.

3. **Khe ho rang.** Khe ho rang trong dong co gay tre theo doi; su dung rang chong khe ho hoac dan dong truc tiep.

4. **Bang va tuyet.** Tich tu tren dia thay doi can bang va chan tin hieu; can he thong suoi.

5. **Quan cap.** Quay lien tuc can vong truot hoac gioi han quan cap; len ke hoach thoi gian thao quan.

6. **Do phan giai encoder.** Do phan giai encoder kem = theo doi kem; dien hinh can: 0.001° hoac tot hon.

7. **Nham lan LEO vs GEO.** Phan cung theo doi GEO thuong khong xu ly duoc toc do LEO; xac minh thong so.

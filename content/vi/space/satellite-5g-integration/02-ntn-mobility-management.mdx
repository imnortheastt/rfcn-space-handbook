## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Trong LEO NTN, ve tinh di chuyen voi van toc 7 km/s—mot cell bao phu ban bay gio se bien mat trong 60-90 giay. Handover truyen thong, duoc thiet ke cho nguoi dung di bo va cell tinh, that bai duoi dong luc hoc nay. 3GPP da gioi thieu conditional handover (CHO) va co che thay doi cell du doan dac biet cho NTN. Hieu nhung dieu nay la can thiet de thiet ke ket noi ve tinh dang tin cay.

## Ap dung

**Du doan thoi gian handover:**

```python
import numpy as np
from dataclasses import dataclass

@dataclass
class Satellite:
    id: int
    lat: float  # vi do diem duoi ve tinh
    lon: float  # kinh do diem duoi ve tinh
    altitude_km: float
    velocity_km_s: float
    heading_deg: float  # huong di chuyen

def elevation_angle(ue_lat, ue_lon, sat_lat, sat_lon, sat_alt_km):
    """Tinh goc nang tu UE den ve tinh."""
    earth_radius_km = 6371
    
    # Goc phan cach (don gian hoa hinh cau)
    lat1, lat2 = np.radians(ue_lat), np.radians(sat_lat)
    dlon = np.radians(sat_lon - ue_lon)
    
    central_angle = np.arccos(
        np.sin(lat1) * np.sin(lat2) + 
        np.cos(lat1) * np.cos(lat2) * np.cos(dlon)
    )
    
    # Goc nang
    slant_range = np.sqrt(
        earth_radius_km**2 + (earth_radius_km + sat_alt_km)**2 - 
        2 * earth_radius_km * (earth_radius_km + sat_alt_km) * np.cos(central_angle)
    )
    
    elevation = np.arcsin(
        ((earth_radius_km + sat_alt_km) * np.cos(central_angle) - earth_radius_km) / 
        slant_range
    )
    
    return np.degrees(elevation)

def predict_handover_times(ue_lat, ue_lon, satellites, min_elevation_deg=10):
    """
    Du doan trinh tu handover cho UE voi chom sao ve tinh da cho.
    
    Tra ve danh sach (time_s, serving_sat_id, target_sat_id, trigger_elevation)
    """
    handovers = []
    current_serving = None
    
    # Mo phong trong 10 phut
    for t in range(0, 600, 1):  # buoc 1 giay
        elevations = {}
        
        for sat in satellites:
            # Lan truyen tuyen tinh don gian diem duoi ve tinh
            new_lat = sat.lat + (sat.velocity_km_s * t / 111) * np.cos(np.radians(sat.heading_deg))
            new_lon = sat.lon + (sat.velocity_km_s * t / 111) * np.sin(np.radians(sat.heading_deg)) / np.cos(np.radians(sat.lat))
            
            elev = elevation_angle(ue_lat, ue_lon, new_lat, new_lon, sat.altitude_km)
            if elev > min_elevation_deg:
                elevations[sat.id] = elev
        
        if not elevations:
            continue
            
        # Tim ve tinh tot nhat (goc nang cao nhat)
        best_sat = max(elevations.keys(), key=lambda x: elevations[x])
        
        if current_serving is None:
            current_serving = best_sat
        elif best_sat != current_serving:
            # Dieu kien handover: goc nang muc tieu vuot phuc vu theo bien
            if elevations.get(best_sat, 0) > elevations.get(current_serving, 0) + 5:
                handovers.append({
                    'time_s': t,
                    'from_sat': current_serving,
                    'to_sat': best_sat,
                    'from_elev': elevations.get(current_serving, 0),
                    'to_elev': elevations.get(best_sat, 0)
                })
                current_serving = best_sat
    
    return handovers

# Vi du: Mo phong chom sao Walker
print("Vi du Du doan Handover NTN")
print("=" * 55)

# Tao chom sao ve tinh don gian (3 ve tinh trong cung mat phang)
satellites = [
    Satellite(id=47, lat=30, lon=-10, altitude_km=550, velocity_km_s=7.5, heading_deg=80),
    Satellite(id=48, lat=25, lon=5, altitude_km=550, velocity_km_s=7.5, heading_deg=80),
    Satellite(id=49, lat=20, lon=20, altitude_km=550, velocity_km_s=7.5, heading_deg=80),
]

# UE tai vi tri co dinh
ue_lat, ue_lon = 35, 0

handovers = predict_handover_times(ue_lat, ue_lon, satellites)

print(f"\nVi tri UE: ({ue_lat}°B, {ue_lon}°D)")
print(f"Chom sao: 3 ve tinh LEO tai 550 km")
print(f"\nTrinh tu handover du doan:")
print("-" * 55)

for ho in handovers[:5]:  # Hien thi 5 dau tien
    print(f"  T+{ho['time_s']:3d}s: Sat {ho['from_sat']} → Sat {ho['to_sat']}")
    print(f"         Goc nang: {ho['from_elev']:.1f}° → {ho['to_elev']:.1f}°")

# Tinh toan thoi gian cau hinh CHO
print("\n" + "=" * 55)
print("Cau hinh CHO (tinh truoc):")
print("-" * 55)

for i, ho in enumerate(handovers[:3]):
    # Gui cau hinh CHO 30 giay truoc handover
    config_time = max(0, ho['time_s'] - 30)
    print(f"  Config {i+1}: Gui tai T+{config_time}s, thuc hien tai T+{ho['time_s']}s")
    print(f"            Muc tieu: Sat {ho['to_sat']}, dieu kien: dua tren thoi gian")
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Het han cau hinh CHO.** Handover cau hinh truoc tro nen cu; can co che lam moi.

2. **Do phuc tap da bup song.** Cell co dinh trai dat voi lai bup song them do phuc tap timing.

3. **Kha dung ISL.** Khong phai tat ca chom sao co ISL; chuyen ngu canh co the qua mat dat.

4. **Tac dong mat GNSS.** Khong GNSS = khong vi tri = du doan handover suy giam.

5. **Gian doan Doppler.** Handover thay doi dau Doppler; UE phai thu lai nhanh chong.

6. **Can Cell ID.** Cell co dinh ve tinh quay vong qua nhieu Cell ID moi quy dao.

7. **Khong khop timer.** Timer thuc hien CHO phai tinh den do tre lan truyen NTN.

## Khai niem co ban

### Quy dao dia tinh (GEO) co ban

Ve tinh VINASAT nam tren quy dao dia tinh o do cao ~35,786 km. O do cao nay, chu ky quy dao bang chu ky tu quay cua Trai Dat (23h 56m 4s thoi gian sao), lam cho ve tinh dung yen so voi tram mat dat.

**Tai sao GEO cho truyen thong?**
- Mot ve tinh phu song ~1/3 be mat Trai Dat
- Khong can anten theo doi (chi huong co dinh)
- Phu song lien tuc 24/7 cho phat song/vien thong
- Danh doi: Do tre cao (~240ms mot chieu, ~480ms khu hoi)

### Tong quan doi tau VINASAT

| Thong so | VINASAT-1 | VINASAT-2 |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Ngay phong | 18/04/2008 | 16/05/2012 |
| Vi tri quy dao | 132В°E | 131.8В°E |
| Nha san xuat | Lockheed Martin | Lockheed Martin |

| Ten lua phong | Ariane 5 ECA | Ariane 5 ECA |

| Tuoi tho thiet ke | 15 nam | 15 nam |
| Van hanh | VNPT/VTC | VNPT |

## Phan tich Link Budget

### Vi du thuc te: Tram VSAT den VINASAT-1

Tinh C/NвӮҖ duong xuong cho tram VSAT nhan tu VINASAT-1.

**Cac thong so cho truoc:**
- EIRP ve tinh: 39 dBW
- Tan so: 4.0 GHz (C-band downlink)
- Chieu dai duong truyen: 38,000 km (tam nghieng dien hinh)
- Anten thu: dia 2.4m, hieu suat 60%
- Nhiet do tap am he thong: 75 K

```python
import math

def link_budget_vinasat():
    """
    Tinh C/N0 duong xuong C-band VINASAT.
    """
    # Thong so cho truoc
    eirp_dbw = 39.0          # EIRP ve tinh theo dBW
    freq_ghz = 4.0           # Tan so downlink
    distance_km = 38000      # Tam nghieng den ve tinh
    dish_diameter_m = 2.4    # Duong kinh anten thu
    antenna_efficiency = 0.6 # Hieu suat dien hinh
    system_temp_k = 75       # Nhiet do tap am he thong
    
    # Hang so
    c = 3e8                  # Toc do anh sang (m/s)
    k_boltzmann = 1.38e-23   # Hang so Boltzmann (J/K)
    
    # Tinh suy hao khong gian tu do
    wavelength_m = c / (freq_ghz * 1e9)
    fspl_db = 20 * math.log10(4 * math.pi * distance_km * 1e3 / wavelength_m)
    
    # Tinh do loi anten thu
    aperture_area = math.pi * (dish_diameter_m / 2) ** 2
    effective_area = antenna_efficiency * aperture_area
    gain_linear = (4 * math.pi * effective_area) / (wavelength_m ** 2)
    gain_db = 10 * math.log10(gain_linear)
    
    # Tinh G/T (chi so chat luong thu)
    noise_temp_db = 10 * math.log10(system_temp_k)
    g_over_t_db = gain_db - noise_temp_db
    
    # Tinh cong suat nhan
    received_power_dbw = eirp_dbw - fspl_db + gain_db
    
    # Tinh C/N0
    k_db = 10 * math.log10(k_boltzmann)  # -228.6 dB(W/Hz/K)
    c_over_n0_db = eirp_dbw - fspl_db + g_over_t_db - k_db
    
    print("=== Link Budget Duong xuong C-Band VINASAT ===")
    print(f"EIRP ve tinh:          {eirp_dbw:8.2f} dBW")
    print(f"Suy hao khong gian:    {fspl_db:8.2f} dB")
    print(f"Do loi anten thu:      {gain_db:8.2f} dBi")
    print(f"Nhiet do tap am:       {system_temp_k:8.1f} K ({noise_temp_db:.1f} dBK)")
    print(f"G/T:                   {g_over_t_db:8.2f} dB/K")
    print(f"Cong suat nhan:        {received_power_dbw:8.2f} dBW")
    print(f"C/N0:                  {c_over_n0_db:8.2f} dB-Hz")
    
    # C/N0 yeu cau cho DVB-S2 QPSK 1/2: ~63 dB-Hz
    margin = c_over_n0_db - 63
    print(f"Du tru lien ket (vs 63 dB-Hz): {margin:.2f} dB")
    
    return c_over_n0_db

if __name__ == "__main__":
    link_budget_vinasat()
```

### Anh huong suy hao mua

Tin hieu Ku-band chiu suy hao mua dang ke o khi hau nhiet doi:

| Cuong do mua (mm/h) | Suy hao Ku-band | Suy hao C-band |
|---------------------|-----------------|----------------|
| 10 | 2-3 dB | 0.2 dB |
| 50 | 8-12 dB | 0.8 dB |
| 100 | 15-25 dB | 1.5 dB |

**Quy tac thiet ke:** De dat 99.5% kha dung o Viet Nam, them 6-8 dB du tru mua cho Ku-band.

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Nhung sai lam thuong gap

### 1. Bo qua anh huong khi quyen
C-band thuong duoc chon thay vi Ku-band o vung nhiet doi du mat do bang thong thap hon vi kha nang chong suy hao mua.

### 2. Nham lan vi tri quy dao
VINASAT-1 (132В°E) va VINASAT-2 (131.8В°E) chi cach nhau 0.2В°. O khoang cach GEO, day la ~140 km khoang cach vat ly. Anten mat dat can co kha nang phan biet du.

### 3. Du toan nhien lieu cuoi doi
Duy tri vi tri tieu thu ~95% ngan sach nhien lieu. Khi ve tinh qua 15 nam, nha dieu hanh co the cho phep troi co kiem soat de keo dai tuoi tho voi chi phi dieu chinh huong anten mat dat lon hon.

### 4. Sai lech phan cuc
VINASAT su dung ca phan cuc tuyen tinh (ngang/doc) va tron tuy theo transponder. Sai lech phan cuc gay mat 20-30 dB.

</section>

## Cau noi sang bai hoc tiep

VINASAT dai dien cho buoc nhap cuoc cua Viet Nam vao truyen thong GEO. Bai hoc tiep theo se noi ve **MicroDragon**, ve tinh dau tien do Viet Nam tu phat trien tren quy dao thap (LEO), the hien nang luc vu tru noi sinh dang phat trien cua quoc gia cho quan sat Trai Dat thay vi truyen thong.

**So sanh chinh:**
- VINASAT: Nuoc ngoai san xuat, GEO, truyen thong, tuoi tho 15 nam
- MicroDragon: Viet Nam che tao, LEO, quan sat Trai Dat, nhiem vu 2 nam

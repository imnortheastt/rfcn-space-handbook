## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

TCP gia dinh goi tin den dich trong vai mili giay voi cac lien ket on dinh, luon ket noi. Khong gian pha vo moi gia dinh do: do tre tu phut den gio, lien ket bien mat khi hanh tinh xoay, va cac buoc nhay qua ve tinh chuyen tiep co the giu du lieu hang gio. Mang Chiu tre (DTN) duoc xay dung dac biet cho moi truong nay—day la kien truc mang lam cho truyen du lieu lien hanh tinh thuc su hoat dong. Hieu DTN la thiet yeu cho bat ky ai xay dung he thong thong tin cislunar hoac khong gian sau.

## Ap dung

**Phan tich thong luong va luu tru DTN:**

```python
import numpy as np

def dung_luong_lien_lac_mb(thoi_gian_phut, toc_do_mbps, hieu_suat=0.85):
    """Tinh dung luong du lieu co the truyen trong cua so lien lac."""
    return thoi_gian_phut * 60 * toc_do_mbps * hieu_suat / 8

def ngay_de_truyen(tong_mb, lien_lac_moi_ngay):
    """Tinh so ngay can de truyen du lieu voi danh sach lien lac."""
    dung_luong_hang_ngay = sum(c['dung_luong_mb'] for c in lien_lac_moi_ngay)
    return tong_mb / dung_luong_hang_ngay if dung_luong_hang_ngay > 0 else float('inf')

# Vi du: lien lac mang chuyen tiep Sao Hoa
print("Phan tich Tra Du lieu DTN")
print("=" * 60)

# Dinh nghia lien lac hang ngay dien hinh cho rover Sao Hoa
lien_lac_rover = [
    {"ten": "Rover→MRO (lan 1)", "thoi_gian_phut": 8, "toc_do_mbps": 2},
    {"ten": "Rover→MRO (lan 2)", "thoi_gian_phut": 6, "toc_do_mbps": 2},
    {"ten": "Rover→MAVEN", "thoi_gian_phut": 10, "toc_do_mbps": 1},
]

lien_lac_chuyen_tiep = [
    {"ten": "MRO→DSN", "thoi_gian_phut": 75, "toc_do_mbps": 6},
    {"ten": "MAVEN→DSN", "thoi_gian_phut": 45, "toc_do_mbps": 4},
]

print("\n1. Cua so Lien lac Hang ngay")
print("-" * 60)

rover_hang_ngay = 0
chuyen_tiep_hang_ngay = 0

print("Be mat-den-Chuyen tiep:")
for c in lien_lac_rover:
    dl = dung_luong_lien_lac_mb(c["thoi_gian_phut"], c["toc_do_mbps"])
    c["dung_luong_mb"] = dl
    rover_hang_ngay += dl
    print(f"  {c['ten']:<25} {c['thoi_gian_phut']:>3} phut x {c['toc_do_mbps']} Mbps = {dl:.0f} MB")

print(f"\n  Tong tai len be mat: {rover_hang_ngay:.0f} MB/sol")

print("\nChuyen tiep-den-T.Dat:")
for c in lien_lac_chuyen_tiep:
    dl = dung_luong_lien_lac_mb(c["thoi_gian_phut"], c["toc_do_mbps"])
    c["dung_luong_mb"] = dl
    chuyen_tiep_hang_ngay += dl
    print(f"  {c['ten']:<25} {c['thoi_gian_phut']:>3} phut x {c['toc_do_mbps']} Mbps = {dl:.0f} MB")

print(f"\n  Tong tai xuong T.Dat: {chuyen_tiep_hang_ngay:.0f} MB/sol")

# Phan tich nut co chai
print("\n2. Phan tich Nut Co chai")
print("-" * 60)

nut_co_chai = min(rover_hang_ngay, chuyen_tiep_hang_ngay)
print(f"  Dung luong be mat-chuyen tiep: {rover_hang_ngay:.0f} MB/sol")
print(f"  Dung luong chuyen tiep-T.Dat:  {chuyen_tiep_hang_ngay:.0f} MB/sol")
print(f"  Nut co chai he thong:          {nut_co_chai:.0f} MB/sol")

if rover_hang_ngay < chuyen_tiep_hang_ngay:
    print("  → Nut co chai la be mat-chuyen tiep (dien hinh)")
else:
    print("  → Nut co chai la chuyen tiep-T.Dat")

# Tra du lieu nhiem vu
print("\n3. Uoc tinh Tra Du lieu Nhiem vu")
print("-" * 60)

muc_tieu_khoa_hoc = [
    ("Hinh anh sol (dien hinh)", 500),
    ("Bo nho phat hien lon", 2000),
    ("Video truc thang (10 phut)", 800),
    ("Tai lieu bo nho mau", 5000),
]

for ten, kich_thuoc_mb in muc_tieu_khoa_hoc:
    sol = kich_thuoc_mb / nut_co_chai
    print(f"  {ten:<30} {kich_thuoc_mb:>5} MB → {sol:.1f} sol")

# Yeu cau luu tru
print("\n4. Yeu cau Luu tru Nut")
print("-" * 60)

khoang_cach_xau_nhat_gio = 12  # Neu MRO di qua rover, roi doi DSN
luu_tru_toi_da = rover_hang_ngay  # Luong mot sol

print(f"  Khoang cach T.Dat xau nhat: {khoang_cach_xau_nhat_gio} gio")
print(f"  Tich luy du lieu toi da: {luu_tru_toi_da:.0f} MB")
print(f"  Luu tru chuyen tiep khuyen nghi: {luu_tru_toi_da * 1.5:.0f} MB (du 50%)")

# Hieu suat LTP
print("\n5. Hieu suat LTP vs TCP")
print("-" * 60)

thoi_gian_as_mot_chieu_giay = 12.5 * 60  # Khoang cach Sao Hoa trung binh
rtt_giay = 2 * thoi_gian_as_mot_chieu_giay
toc_do_lien_ket_mbps = 2
kich_thuoc_khoi_mb = 10

# TCP: doi ACK sau moi doan (don gian hoa)
tcp_doan = kich_thuoc_khoi_mb * 8 / 0.001472  # doan ~1500 byte
tcp_thoi_gian = tcp_doan * rtt_giay / 60  # phut

# LTP: gui tat ca, roi diem kiem tra
ltp_thoi_gian_truyen = kich_thuoc_khoi_mb * 8 / toc_do_lien_ket_mbps  # giay
ltp_thoi_gian = (ltp_thoi_gian_truyen + rtt_giay) / 60  # phut (mot RTT cho bao cao)

print(f"  Thoi gian anh sang mot chieu: {thoi_gian_as_mot_chieu_giay/60:.1f} phut")
print(f"  Thoi gian khu hoi: {rtt_giay/60:.1f} phut")
print(f"  Kich thuoc khoi: {kich_thuoc_khoi_mb} MB")
print()
print(f"  Cach TCP (ACK tung doan): Se mat ~mai mai")
print(f"  Cach LTP (dua tren diem kiem tra): {ltp_thoi_gian:.1f} phut")
print()
print("  LTP la thiet yeu cho lien ket khong gian!")

# Tinh toan thoi gian song bundle
print("\n6. Cac Xem xet Thoi gian song Bundle")
print("-" * 60)

kich_ban = [
    ("Chuyen tiep Mat Trang", 3, 1),      # 3 ngay, 1 ngay dien hinh
    ("Sao Hoa (doi dien)", 22, 7),  # 22 phut OWLT, 7 ngay dien hinh
    ("Sao Hoa (giao hoi)", 22, 30),  # Giao hoi mat troi = khong TT
    ("Hanh tinh ngoai", 60*4, 90),  # 4 gio OWLT, 90 ngay
]

print(f"  {'Kich ban':<25} {'TG song Dien hinh':>18}")
print("-" * 60)

for ten, owlt_phut, thoi_gian_song_ngay in kich_ban:
    print(f"  {ten:<25} {thoi_gian_song_ngay:>15} ngay")

print("\n  Quy tac: TG song > do tre toi da du kien den dich")
print("  Qua ngan = du lieu bi bo; qua dai = luu tru day")
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Dung DTN khi TCP hoat dong.** DTN co overhead. Cho LEO voi lien ket on dinh, giao thuc tieu chuan co the don gian hon.

2. **Danh gia thap nhu cau luu tru.** Chuyen giao giam ho nghia la cac nut phai luu du lieu—doi khi hang ngay. Dinh kich thuoc luu tru cho khoang cach lien lac xau nhat.

3. **Bo qua cap nhat do thi lien lac.** Co hoc quy dao co the du doan, nhung bat thuong tau vu tru va thay doi lich DSN yeu cau cap nhat CGR.

4. **Thoi gian song bundle sai.** Qua ngan: du lieu chet truoc khi giao. Qua dai: luu tru day bundle cu.

5. **Quen BPSec.** Bao mat Bundle Protocol la thiet yeu—bundle co the di qua cac nut khong tin cay qua nhieu tuan.

6. **Gia dinh giam ho ngay lap tuc.** Chuyen giao giam ho yeu cau chap nhan ro rang. Khong xoa du lieu nguon cho den khi xac nhan.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Ve tinh HTS khong chi la bup song diem va tai su dung tan so—no la he thong hoan chinh dinh tuyen hang tram gigabit giua cac cong va bup nguoi dung. Kien truc tai huu ich quyet dinh tinh linh hoat, dung luong va kha nang phuc hoi. Hieu bent-pipe vs tai tao, nhay bup va da dang cong phan biet giua biet tiep thi HTS va thuc te ky thuat.

## Ap dung

**Dinh co tai huu ich HTS:**

```python
import numpy as np

def hts_capacity_estimate(num_user_beams, bw_per_beam_mhz, spectral_eff):
    """
    Uoc tinh tong dung luong HTS
    
    spectral_eff: dien hinh 2-4 bits/s/Hz
    """
    capacity_gbps = num_user_beams * bw_per_beam_mhz * spectral_eff / 1000
    return capacity_gbps

def gateway_count(total_capacity_gbps, capacity_per_gw_gbps, redundancy_ratio=1.3):
    """
    Uoc tinh so cong can thiet
    
    redundancy_ratio: N+M trong do ty le = (N+M)/N
    """
    active_gws = np.ceil(total_capacity_gbps / capacity_per_gw_gbps)
    total_gws = np.ceil(active_gws * redundancy_ratio)
    return int(active_gws), int(total_gws)

def beam_hopping_gain(peak_to_avg_ratio):
    """
    Tang ich dung luong tu nhay bup
    
    peak_to_avg_ratio: bien dong nhu cau luu luong (thuong 2-5)
    """
    # Nhay bup cho phep chia se cong suat dua tren nhu cau
    # Tang ich dung luong tuong duong vs phan bo co dinh
    return np.sqrt(peak_to_avg_ratio)

# Vi du: HTS loai ViaSat-3
print("Vi du Thiet ke Tai huu ich HTS:")
print("=" * 50)

num_beams = 300
bw_per_beam = 250  # MHz (sau tai su dung tan so)
spectral_eff = 3.5  # bits/s/Hz

capacity = hts_capacity_estimate(num_beams, bw_per_beam, spectral_eff)
print(f"Bup nguoi dung: {num_beams}")
print(f"Bang thong moi bup: {bw_per_beam} MHz")
print(f"Hieu suat pho: {spectral_eff} bits/s/Hz")
print(f"Tong dung luong: {capacity:.0f} Gbps")

# Lap ke hoach cong
gw_capacity = 10  # Gbps moi cong
active, total = gateway_count(capacity, gw_capacity)
print(f"\nHa tang cong:")
print(f"  Dung luong moi cong: {gw_capacity} Gbps")
print(f"  Cong hoat dong: {active}")
print(f"  Tong voi du phong: {total}")

# Loi ich nhay bup
peak_avg = 3.0
gain = beam_hopping_gain(peak_avg)
print(f"\nNhay bup:")
print(f"  Ti le dinh-tren-trung-binh luu luong: {peak_avg}")
print(f"  Tang ich dung luong hieu qua: {gain:.1f}×")
print(f"  Dung luong co dinh tuong duong: {capacity * gain:.0f} Gbps")
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Dung luong tiep thi.** Gbps quang cao gia dinh dieu kien tot nhat; thong luong thuc te thap hon.

2. **Nut that cong.** Moi cong can backhaul cap quang—kha dung va chi phi thay doi theo vi tri.

3. **Dong bo nhay bup.** Thiet bi phai dong bo voi lich nhay; them do phuc tap.

4. **Tieu thu nang luong OBP.** Tai huu ich tai tao tieu thu nhieu dien DC hon dang ke.

5. **Rui ro cong nghe.** Cong nghe OBP co dinh khi phong; bent-pipe thich ung voi nang cap mat dat.

6. **He so lap day.** Su dung dung luong hiem khi vuot 30-50% trong thuc te.

7. **Phoi hop nhieu.** Nhieu HTS trong cung slot tao ra chia se tan so phuc tap.

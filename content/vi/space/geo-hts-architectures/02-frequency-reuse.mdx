## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Pho tan so dat va khan hiem. Ve tinh truyen thong su dung moi tan so mot lan. Ve tinh HTS tai su dung cung tan so qua nhieu bup song diem—bien 500 MHz pho thanh 5+ GHz bang thong hieu qua. Ky thuat nay giong het mang di dong nhung hoat dong o quy mo quy dao.

</section>

## Ap dung

**Tinh toan tai su dung tan so:**

```python
import numpy as np

def frequency_reuse_capacity(system_bw_mhz, num_colors, num_beams):
    """
    Tinh bang thong hieu qua voi tai su dung tan so
    
    Tra ve: dict voi cac chi so dung luong
    """
    bw_per_beam = system_bw_mhz / num_colors
    effective_bw = bw_per_beam * num_beams
    reuse_gain = num_beams / num_colors
    
    return {
        'bw_moi_bup_mhz': bw_per_beam,
        'bw_hieu_qua_mhz': effective_bw,
        'he_so_tai_su_dung': reuse_gain
    }

def carrier_to_interference(antenna_gain_db, sidelobe_level_db, 
                            reuse_distance_beamwidths, num_interferers=6):
    """
    Uoc tinh C/I cho mau luc giac
    
    reuse_distance_beamwidths: khoang cach den bup cung mau gan nhat
                               tinh theo don vi do rong bup
    """
    # Don gian hoa: nhieu tu bup phu cua cac bup dong kenh
    # O khoang cach D, do thi anten cung cap cach ly
    
    # Nguon nhieu tang 1: thuong 6 trong mang luc giac
    # Moi cai o khoang cach D, dong gop qua muc bup phu
    
    # Chenh lech ton hao duong truyen (don gian, gia dinh trai dat phang)
    # Cong suat giam theo 1/khoang cach^2 trong khong gian tu do
    distance_loss_db = 20 * np.log10(reuse_distance_beamwidths)
    
    # C/I moi nguon nhieu = khuech dai bup chinh - bup phu - he so khoang cach
    single_ci = antenna_gain_db - sidelobe_level_db - distance_loss_db
    
    # Nhieu nguon nhieu (tong cong suat)
    aggregate_ci = single_ci - 10 * np.log10(num_interferers)
    
    return aggregate_ci

def polarization_isolation(cross_pol_db=25):
    """
    Cach ly them tu phan cuc vuong goc
    
    Gia tri dien hinh: 25-30 dB cho phan cuc tron
    """
    return cross_pol_db

# Vi du: Thiet ke HTS Ka-band
print("Phan tich Tai su dung Tan so:")
print("=" * 50)

# Tham so he thong
system_bw = 500  # MHz
num_beams = 100

for n_colors in [7, 4, 3]:
    result = frequency_reuse_capacity(system_bw, n_colors, num_beams)
    print(f"\nMau tai su dung N={n_colors}:")
    print(f"  Bang thong moi bup: {result['bw_moi_bup_mhz']:.1f} MHz")
    print(f"  Bang thong hieu qua: {result['bw_hieu_qua_mhz']/1000:.1f} GHz")
    print(f"  He so tai su dung: {result['he_so_tai_su_dung']:.1f}×")

# Uoc tinh C/I
print("\n" + "=" * 50)
print("Uoc tinh C/I (mau 4 mau):")

# Tham so anten dien hinh
main_beam_gain = 45  # dBi
sidelobe_level = -25  # dB tuong doi voi dinh
reuse_distance = 2.0  # do rong bup den dong kenh gan nhat

ci = carrier_to_interference(main_beam_gain, sidelobe_level, 
                             reuse_distance, num_interferers=6)
print(f"  C/I (chi khong gian): {ci:.1f} dB")

# Voi phan biet phan cuc
ci_with_pol = ci + polarization_isolation(25)
print(f"  C/I (voi cach ly phan cuc): {ci_with_pol:.1f} dB")
```

**So sanh mau tai su dung:**

| Mau | Mau | He so Tai su dung | Du tru C/I | Do phuc tap |
|-----|-----|-------------------|------------|-------------|
| N=7 | 7 | Thap | Cao (~25dB) | Don gian |
| N=4 | 4 | Trung binh | Tot (~18dB) | Tieu chuan |
| N=3 | 3 | Cao | Thap hon (~15dB) | Trung binh |
| N=1 | 1 | Toi da | Can IC | Phuc tap |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Bo qua bup phu.** Muc bup phu anten truc tiep anh huong C/I dat duoc.

2. **Bien dang bup bien.** Bup o bien vung phu co hinh hoc khac—co the can tai su dung khac.

3. **Tan xa mua.** Mua co the tan xa tin hieu giua cac bup, giam cach ly.

4. **Phan cuc cheo.** Anten thuc co phan cuc cheo khong hoan hao; dung gia dinh 30dB ly tuong.

5. **Diem nong luu luong.** Cung tai su dung o moi noi bo qua nhu cau thay doi theo khong gian.

6. **Tong hop nhieu.** Nhieu bup cung mau deu dong gop—dung chi xet mot.

7. **Duong ve cong.** Lien ket cong cung can lap ke hoach tan so; khong the bo qua phia cap.

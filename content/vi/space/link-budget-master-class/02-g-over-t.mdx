## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

G/T la con so duy nhat dac trung cho kha nang may thu cua ban—no nhay cam the nao voi cac tin hieu yeu. G/T cao nghia la ban co the dong lien ket voi cong suat phat thap hon hoac anten nho hon o dau kia. Day la doi trong cua EIRP cho may thu, va cung nhau chung quyet dinh lieu lien ket cua ban co hoat dong khong. Hieu G/T giup ban thiet ke tram mat dat, chi dinh may thu ve tinh, va xu ly su co tin hieu yeu.

## Ap dung

**Tinh toan G/T:**

```python
import numpy as np

def nf_sang_nhietdo(nf_db, t_ref=290):
    """Chuyen he so tap am (dB) sang nhiet do tap am tuong duong (K)."""
    f_tuyen_tinh = 10 ** (nf_db / 10)
    return t_ref * (f_tuyen_tinh - 1)

def nhietdo_sang_nf(nhietdo_k, t_ref=290):
    """Chuyen nhiet do tap am (K) sang he so tap am (dB)."""
    f_tuyen_tinh = nhietdo_k / t_ref + 1
    return 10 * np.log10(f_tuyen_tinh)

def cascade_nhietdo_tapam(cac_tang):
    """
    Tinh nhiet do tap am cascade.
    
    Args:
        cac_tang: Danh sach cac tuple (do_loi_db, nhietdo_tapam_k)
    
    Returns:
        Tong nhiet do tap am he thong tinh bang K
    """
    t_tong = 0
    do_loi_tich_luy = 1
    
    for do_loi_db, nhietdo_k in cac_tang:
        t_tong += nhietdo_k / do_loi_tich_luy
        do_loi_tich_luy *= 10 ** (do_loi_db / 10)
    
    return t_tong

def do_loi_parabol_dbi(duong_kinh_m, tan_so_ghz, hieu_suat=0.55):
    """Tinh do loi anten parabol tinh bang dBi."""
    buoc_song_m = 0.3 / tan_so_ghz
    do_loi_tuyen_tinh = hieu_suat * (np.pi * duong_kinh_m / buoc_song_m) ** 2
    return 10 * np.log10(do_loi_tuyen_tinh)

def tinh_g_tren_t(do_loi_dbi, t_sys_k):
    """Tinh G/T tinh bang dB/K."""
    return do_loi_dbi - 10 * np.log10(t_sys_k)

# Vi du tinh toan
print("Tinh toan G/T")
print("=" * 60)

# Vi du tram mat dat
print("\n1. Tram mat dat Ku-band (12 GHz)")
print("-" * 60)

# Anten
duong_kinh_dia = 3.0  # m
tan_so = 12.0  # GHz
do_loi_anten = do_loi_parabol_dbi(duong_kinh_dia, tan_so)
print(f"  Do loi anten: {do_loi_anten:.1f} dBi ({duong_kinh_dia} m @ {tan_so} GHz)")

# Dong gop tap am
t_troi = 25  # K (troi trong, goc nang 30°)
t_dat = 30  # K (nhan bup phu)
t_anten = t_troi + t_dat
print(f"  T_anten: {t_anten} K (troi {t_troi}K + dat {t_dat}K)")

# Bo tiep va LNA
ton_hao_tiep_db = 0.2
t_tiep = nf_sang_nhietdo(ton_hao_tiep_db)
print(f"  Ton hao bo tiep: {ton_hao_tiep_db} dB → T_tiep = {t_tiep:.0f} K")

lna_nf = 0.8  # dB
t_lna = nf_sang_nhietdo(lna_nf)
print(f"  NF LNA: {lna_nf} dB → T_LNA = {t_lna:.0f} K")

# Nhiet do he thong
t_sys = t_anten + t_tiep + t_lna
print(f"  T_sys = {t_anten} + {t_tiep:.0f} + {t_lna:.0f} = {t_sys:.0f} K")

# G/T
g_tren_t = tinh_g_tren_t(do_loi_anten, t_sys)
print(f"  G/T = {do_loi_anten:.1f} - 10 log({t_sys:.0f}) = {g_tren_t:.1f} dB/K")

# Vi du may thu ve tinh
print("\n2. May thu Ve tinh Ku-band")
print("-" * 60)

vt_do_loi = 35  # dBi (anten nho hon)
vt_t_anten = 290  # K (nhin vao Trai dat)
vt_lna_nf = 2.0  # dB (LNA am hon)
vt_t_lna = nf_sang_nhietdo(vt_lna_nf)
vt_t_sys = vt_t_anten + vt_t_lna

print(f"  Do loi anten: {vt_do_loi} dBi")
print(f"  T_anten: {vt_t_anten} K (nhin vao Trai dat)")
print(f"  NF LNA: {vt_lna_nf} dB → T_LNA = {vt_t_lna:.0f} K")
print(f"  T_sys = {vt_t_sys:.0f} K")

vt_g_tren_t = tinh_g_tren_t(vt_do_loi, vt_t_sys)
print(f"  G/T = {vt_g_tren_t:.1f} dB/K")

# Vi du cascade
print("\n3. Cascade Chuoi May thu")
print("-" * 60)

cac_tang = [
    (30, 50),   # LNA: do loi 30 dB, tap am 50 K
    (-3, 150),  # Cap: -3 dB (ton hao), 150 K
    (10, 1000), # Tron: do loi 10 dB, tap am 1000 K
]

print("  Tang       | Do loi (dB) | T (K)")
print("-" * 40)
for i, (g, t) in enumerate(cac_tang):
    print(f"  Tang {i+1}     |    {g:+5.0f}   |  {t:4.0f}")

t_cascade = cascade_nhietdo_tapam(cac_tang)
print("-" * 40)
print(f"  T_tong = {t_cascade:.1f} K")
print(f"  (Tang dau tien chiem uu the!)")

# Gia tri G/T dien hinh
print("\n4. Gia tri G/T Dien hinh")
print("-" * 60)
print(f"  {'He thong':<30} {'G/T (dB/K)':<15}")
print("-" * 60)

he_thong = [
    ("Gateway lon (10m, Ku)", 40),
    ("Tram mat dat trung binh (3m)", 27),
    ("VSAT nho (1.2m)", 18),
    ("Dien thoai ve tinh cam tay", -12),
    ("Ve tinh GEO (bup Trai dat)", 2),
    ("Ve tinh chom sao LEO", 8),
]

for ten, gt in he_thong:
    print(f"  {ten:<30} {gt:>+6.0f}")
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Bo qua ton hao bo tiep.** Ton hao truoc LNA cong truc tiep vao nhiet do tap am.

2. **Sai nhiet do troi.** Phu thuoc tan so, goc nang, va thoi tiet. Dung ITU-R P.372.

3. **Quen nhan mat dat.** Bup phu nhin thay Trai dat am (290 K) ngay ca khi bup chinh nhin troi lanh.

4. **Dung NF thay vi T.** Cho he thong tap am thap, luon lam viec voi nhiet do; NF thieu chinh xac duoi 1 dB.

5. **Gia dinh T_sys khong doi.** Nhiet do troi thay doi theo goc nang—goc nang thap hon = am hon.

6. **Thu tu linh kien quan trong.** Ton hao truoc LNA la tham hoa; cung ton hao sau LNA la khong dang ke.

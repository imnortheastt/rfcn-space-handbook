## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Neu ban da tung xem truyen hinh ve tinh, nhan hinh anh thoi tiet, hoac tai du lieu tu ve tinh thong tin GEO, ban da su dung DVB-S2. Day la tieu chuan cong nghiep cho phat song ve tinh tu 2005, va phien ban ke nhiem DVB-S2X (2014) day hieu suat cao hon nua. Cac tieu chuan nay dinh nghia moi thu tu cac phuong an dieu che den cau truc khung den sua loi—chung la "TCP/IP cua phat song ve tinh." Hieu DVB-S2/S2X la thiet yeu cho bat ky ai lam viec voi thong tin ve tinh thuong mai.

```python

MODCODS = [
    {"ten": "QPSK 1/4",    "dieu_che": "QPSK",   "ty_le": 0.25, "hs": 0.49, "es_n0_db": -2.35},

    {"ten": "QPSK 2/3",    "dieu_che": "QPSK",   "ty_le": 0.67, "hs": 1.32, "es_n0_db": 3.10},
    {"ten": "QPSK 3/4",    "dieu_che": "QPSK",   "ty_le": 0.75, "hs": 1.49, "es_n0_db": 4.03},
    {"ten": "QPSK 5/6",    "dieu_che": "QPSK",   "ty_le": 0.83, "hs": 1.65, "es_n0_db": 4.68},

    {"ten": "8PSK 3/4",    "dieu_che": "8PSK",   "ty_le": 0.75, "hs": 2.23, "es_n0_db": 7.91},
    {"ten": "16APSK 2/3",  "dieu_che": "16APSK", "ty_le": 0.67, "hs": 2.64, "es_n0_db": 9.04},
    {"ten": "16APSK 3/4",  "dieu_che": "16APSK", "ty_le": 0.75, "hs": 2.97, "es_n0_db": 10.21},
    {"ten": "16APSK 5/6",  "dieu_che": "16APSK", "ty_le": 0.83, "hs": 3.30, "es_n0_db": 11.03},
    {"ten": "32APSK 3/4",  "dieu_che": "32APSK", "ty_le": 0.75, "hs": 3.70, "es_n0_db": 12.17},
    {"ten": "32APSK 5/6",  "dieu_che": "32APSK", "ty_le": 0.83, "hs": 4.12, "es_n0_db": 13.05},
    {"ten": "32APSK 8/9",  "dieu_che": "32APSK", "ty_le": 0.89, "hs": 4.40, "es_n0_db": 13.64},
]

def chon_modcod(es_n0_co_san_db, margin_db=1.0):
    """Chon MODCOD tot nhat cho Es/N0 cho truoc voi margin."""
    co_the_dung = es_n0_co_san_db - margin_db
    tot_nhat = None
    for mc in MODCODS:
        if mc["es_n0_db"] <= co_the_dung:
            if tot_nhat is None or mc["hs"] > tot_nhat["hs"]:
                tot_nhat = mc
    return tot_nhat

def tinh_toc_do_du_lieu(toc_do_ky_hieu_msps, modcod, rolloff=0.2):
    """Tinh toc do du lieu cho toc do ky hieu va MODCOD cho truoc."""
    return toc_do_ky_hieu_msps * modcod["hs"]  # Mbps

print("Phan tich MODCOD DVB-S2")
print("=" * 60)

print("\n1. Chon MODCOD theo SNR")
print("-" * 60)

snr_thu = [-2, 0, 3, 6, 9, 12, 14]

print(f"  {'Es/N0':>8} {'MODCOD Da chon':<16} {'Hieu suat':>12}")
print("-" * 60)

for snr in snr_thu:
    mc = chon_modcod(snr)
    if mc:
        print(f"  {snr:>6.1f} dB  {mc['ten']:<16} {mc['hs']:>8.2f} bps/Hz")
    else:
        print(f"  {snr:>6.1f} dB  {'(khong MODCOD)':<<16}")

# Tinh toan dung luong transponder
print("\n2. Phan tich Dung luong Transponder")
print("-" * 60)

bang_thong_transponder_mhz = 36  # Transponder Ku-band dien hinh
rolloffs = [0.35, 0.25, 0.20, 0.15, 0.10, 0.05]

print(f"  Bang thong transponder: {bang_thong_transponder_mhz} MHz")
print()
print(f"  {'Roll-off':>8} {'Toc do Ky hieu':>14} {'Dung luong (32APSK 5/6)':>22}")
print("-" * 60)

modcod_32apsk_56 = MODCODS[-3]  # 32APSK 5/6

for ro in rolloffs:
    toc_do_ky_hieu = bang_thong_transponder_mhz / (1 + ro)
    dung_luong = tinh_toc_do_du_lieu(toc_do_ky_hieu, modcod_32apsk_56)
    dvb = "DVB-S2" if ro >= 0.20 else "DVB-S2X"
    print(f"  {ro*100:>5.0f}%    {toc_do_ky_hieu:>10.2f} Msps    {dung_luong:>8.1f} Mbps ({dvb})")

# Kich ban ACM
print("\n3. Cai thien Dung luong ACM")
print("-" * 60)

# Gia su 10 tram voi dieu kien khac nhau
tram = [
    {"id": 1, "snr": 14, "phan_tram_thoi_gian": 10},  # Dieu kien tot
    {"id": 2, "snr": 12, "phan_tram_thoi_gian": 20},
    {"id": 3, "snr": 10, "phan_tram_thoi_gian": 30},
    {"id": 4, "snr": 8, "phan_tram_thoi_gian": 25},
    {"id": 5, "snr": 5, "phan_tram_thoi_gian": 15},  # Suy giam mua
]

# CCM: thiet ke cho truong hop xau nhat
snr_xau_nhat = min(t["snr"] for t in tram)
ccm_modcod = chon_modcod(snr_xau_nhat)

# ACM: thich ung theo tram
acm_hs_tb = 0
tong_thoi_gian = 0

for t in tram:
    mc = chon_modcod(t["snr"])
    acm_hs_tb += mc["hs"] * t["phan_tram_thoi_gian"] / 100
    tong_thoi_gian += t["phan_tram_thoi_gian"]

print(f"  CCM (thiet ke xau nhat): {ccm_modcod['ten']}")
print(f"    Hieu suat co dinh: {ccm_modcod['hs']:.2f} bps/Hz")
print()
print(f"  ACM (thich ung):")
print(f"    Hieu suat trung binh: {acm_hs_tb:.2f} bps/Hz")
print()

cai_thien = (acm_hs_tb / ccm_modcod['hs'] - 1) * 100
print(f"  Cai thien ACM: +{cai_thien:.1f}%")

# Dinh thoi khung
print("\n4. Dinh thoi Khung")
print("-" * 60)

toc_do_ky_hieu_msps = 30  # 30 Msps dien hinh

ky_hieu_khung_thuong = 33282  # Voi pilot
ky_hieu_khung_ngan = 8370

khung_thuong_us = ky_hieu_khung_thuong / toc_do_ky_hieu_msps
khung_ngan_us = ky_hieu_khung_ngan / toc_do_ky_hieu_msps

print(f"  Toc do ky hieu: {toc_do_ky_hieu_msps} Msps")
print()
print(f"  Khung thuong: {ky_hieu_khung_thuong} ky hieu = {khung_thuong_us:.0f} µs = {khung_thuong_us/1000:.2f} ms")
print(f"  Khung ngan:   {ky_hieu_khung_ngan} ky hieu = {khung_ngan_us:.0f} µs = {khung_ngan_us/1000:.2f} ms")
print()
print(f"  Khung ngan huu ich cho: giong noi do tre thap, dich vu tuong tac")
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Nham lan DVB-S2 voi DVB-S2X.** S2X yeu cau thiet bi tuong thich. Hau het may thu cu chi ho tro S2.

2. **Dung MODCOD cao khong co du margin.** 32APSK 8/9 hieu qua nhung can ~14 dB Es/N0 cong them margin.

3. **Bo qua roll-off trong tinh toan bang thong.** Toc do ky hieu = bang thong / (1 + roll-off). Roll-off nho = dung luong lon hon.

4. **Quen lui HPA cho APSK bac cao.** 32APSK can lui dau ra 2-3 dB, giam cong suat hieu dung.

5. **ACM khong co kenh hoi hop le.** Can bao cao SNR dang tin cay; do cu gay lech MODCOD.

6. **Tron kich thuoc khung giua luong.** May thu can loai khung nhat quan trong Physical Layer Pipe (PLP).

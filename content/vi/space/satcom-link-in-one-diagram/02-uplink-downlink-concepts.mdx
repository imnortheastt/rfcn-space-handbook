## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Duong len va duong xuong khong doi xung—chung su dung cac tan so khac nhau, doi mat voi cac rang buoc cong suat khac nhau, va co moi truong nhieu khac nhau. Hieu su bat doi xung nay la thiet yeu cho ngan sach lien ket, thiet ke thiet bi dau cuoi, va xu ly su co tai sao mot huong hoat dong trong khi huong kia that bai.

</section>

## Ap dung

**So sanh ngan sach duong len va duong xuong:**

```python
import numpy as np

def link_budget(eirp_dbw, distance_km, freq_ghz, g_t_dbk, 
                atm_loss_db=0.5, rain_db=0):
    """Tinh C/N0 cho mot huong lien ket."""
    c = 299792.458
    wavelength_km = c / (freq_ghz * 1e9) / 1000
    fspl = 10 * np.log10((4 * np.pi * distance_km / wavelength_km) ** 2)
    
    total_loss = fspl + atm_loss_db + rain_db
    k = -228.6
    c_n0 = eirp_dbw - total_loss + g_t_dbk - k
    return c_n0, fspl

def combined_cn0(cn0_up, cn0_down):
    """Ket hop C/N0 duong len va xuong cho bent-pipe."""
    linear_up = 10 ** (cn0_up / 10)
    linear_down = 10 ** (cn0_down / 10)
    linear_total = 1 / (1/linear_up + 1/linear_down)
    return 10 * np.log10(linear_total)

# Tham so he thong
distance = 36000  # km (GEO)

# Lien ket di: Gateway → Ve tinh → Nguoi dung
print("LIEN KET DI (Gateway → Nguoi dung)")
print("=" * 55)

# Duong len: Gateway den Ve tinh
gw_eirp = 75  # dBW (dish lon, cong suat cao)
uplink_freq = 14  # GHz
sat_gt = 5  # dB/K (ve tinh nhin Trai dat am)

cn0_up_fwd, fspl_up = link_budget(gw_eirp, distance, uplink_freq, sat_gt)
print(f"Duong len ({uplink_freq} GHz):")
print(f"  Gateway EIRP: {gw_eirp} dBW")
print(f"  G/T ve tinh: {sat_gt} dB/K")
print(f"  Ton hao duong truyen: {fspl_up:.1f} dB")
print(f"  C/N₀: {cn0_up_fwd:.1f} dBHz")

# Duong xuong: Ve tinh den Nguoi dung
sat_eirp = 52  # dBW (transponder)
downlink_freq = 12  # GHz
user_gt = 15  # dB/K (VSAT nho)

cn0_down_fwd, fspl_down = link_budget(sat_eirp, distance, downlink_freq, user_gt)
print(f"\nDuong xuong ({downlink_freq} GHz):")
print(f"  EIRP ve tinh: {sat_eirp} dBW")
print(f"  G/T nguoi dung: {user_gt} dB/K")
print(f"  Ton hao duong truyen: {fspl_down:.1f} dB")
print(f"  C/N₀: {cn0_down_fwd:.1f} dBHz")

cn0_fwd = combined_cn0(cn0_up_fwd, cn0_down_fwd)
print(f"\nC/N₀ Lien ket Di Tong the: {cn0_fwd:.1f} dBHz")
print(f"Bi gioi han boi: {'Duong xuong' if cn0_down_fwd < cn0_up_fwd else 'Duong len'}")

# Lien ket ve: Nguoi dung → Ve tinh → Gateway
print("\n" + "=" * 55)
print("LIEN KET VE (Nguoi dung → Gateway)")
print("=" * 55)

# Duong len: Nguoi dung den Ve tinh
user_eirp = 45  # dBW (thiet bi dau cuoi nho)
cn0_up_ret, _ = link_budget(user_eirp, distance, uplink_freq, sat_gt)
print(f"Duong len ({uplink_freq} GHz):")
print(f"  EIRP nguoi dung: {user_eirp} dBW")
print(f"  G/T ve tinh: {sat_gt} dB/K")
print(f"  C/N₀: {cn0_up_ret:.1f} dBHz")

# Duong xuong: Ve tinh den Gateway
gw_gt = 35  # dB/K (dish gateway lon)
cn0_down_ret, _ = link_budget(sat_eirp, distance, downlink_freq, gw_gt)
print(f"\nDuong xuong ({downlink_freq} GHz):")
print(f"  EIRP ve tinh: {sat_eirp} dBW")
print(f"  G/T gateway: {gw_gt} dB/K")
print(f"  C/N₀: {cn0_down_ret:.1f} dBHz")

cn0_ret = combined_cn0(cn0_up_ret, cn0_down_ret)
print(f"\nC/N₀ Lien ket Ve Tong the: {cn0_ret:.1f} dBHz")
print(f"Bi gioi han boi: {'Duong xuong' if cn0_down_ret < cn0_up_ret else 'Duong len'}")

# So sanh dung luong
print("\n" + "=" * 55)
print("BAT DOI XUNG DUNG LUONG")
print("=" * 55)
capacity_ratio = 10 ** ((cn0_fwd - cn0_ret) / 10)
print(f"Chenh lech C/N₀ Di/Ve: {cn0_fwd - cn0_ret:.1f} dB")
print(f"Ty le dung luong xap xi: {capacity_ratio:.1f}:1")
```

**So sanh bang tan:**

| Bang | Duong len | Duong xuong | Anh huong Mua | Su dung Dien hinh |
|------|-----------|-------------|---------------|-------------------|
| C (4/6 GHz) | 6 GHz | 4 GHz | Thap | Backbone, phat thanh |
| Ku (12/14 GHz) | 14 GHz | 12 GHz | Trung binh | DTH, VSAT |
| Ka (20/30 GHz) | 30 GHz | 20 GHz | Cao | HTS, bang rong |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Gia dinh doi xung.** Lien ket di va ve co ngan sach rat khac nhau.

2. **Bo qua bat doi xung mua.** Tan so duong len cao hon nghia la suy mua nhieu hon; mat dat phai bu.

3. **Quen ket hop bent-pipe.** C/N₀ tong the ≈ yeu hon trong hai lien ket.

4. **Nham G/T ve tinh.** Ve tinh nhin Trai dat am; mat dat nhin bau troi lanh.

5. **Gioi han thiet bi dau cuoi.** Lien ket ve thuong bi nghen boi EIRP thiet bi dau cuoi nho.

6. **Chong lap ke hoach tan so.** Phai giu TX/RX rieng biet de tranh tu-nhieu.

7. **Bo qua dieu khien cong suat.** Dieu khien cong suat duong len thiet yeu de bu suy mua.

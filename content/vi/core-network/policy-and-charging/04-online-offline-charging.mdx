## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Khach hang tra truoc tra tien truoc khi dung dich vu — het credit, dich vu dung. Khach hang tra sau dung truoc, tra sau — dich vu tiep tuc, hoa don den hang thang.

Mang xu ly hai loai nay hoan toan khac nhau. Hieu tinh cuoc online vs offline giai thich tai sao nguoi dung tra truoc bi throttle ngay lap tuc trong khi nguoi dung tra sau thi khong.

</section>

<section class="lesson-apply">

## Ap dung

**Che do tinh cuoc theo tinh huong:**

| Tinh huong | Che do | Tai sao |
|------------|--------|---------|
| SIM tra truoc | Online | Kiem soat credit thoi gian thuc |
| Tra sau khong gioi han | Offline | Khong can kiem tra so du |
| Tra sau co gioi han | Ket hop | Gioi han + billing chi tiet |
| Du lieu duoc tai tro | Online | Ben thu ba tra theo byte |
| Thiet bi IoT | Offline | Billing gop, it overhead |
| Roaming | Online | Kiem soat credit o nuoc ngoai |

**Debug van de tinh cuoc:**

```bash
# Wireshark: online vs offline
http2.header.path contains "chargingData" && json.value.string contains "ONLINE"
http2.header.path contains "chargingData" && json.value.string contains "OFFLINE"

# Kiem tra quota duoc cap
grep "grantedUnit" /var/log/smf/*.log

# Hanh dong don vi cuoi
grep "finalUnitAction" /var/log/chf/*.log

# Dau ra CDR
tail -f /var/spool/chf/cdr/*.cdr
```

**Cac tham so pho bien:**

| Tham so | Online | Offline |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Quota | Cap theo yeu cau | Khong ap dung |
| Bao cao | Tai nguong/thoi gian | Dinh ky/cuoi |
| CDR | Tuy chon | Luon tao |
| Anh huong do tre | Cao hon | Thap hon |
| Xu ly loi | Dich vu bi chan | Dich vu tiep tuc |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Do tre CHF.** Tinh cuoc online them do tre vao moi yeu cau quota. Toi uu thoi gian phan hoi CHF.

2. **Vuot muc giua cap nhat.** Nguoi dung co the vuot quota giua cac cap nhat SMF. Cho phep sai so dung sai.

3. **Het quota giua cuoc goi.** Cat cuoc goi giua cau noi la UX te. Cap quota them cho cuoc goi dang hoat dong.

4. **Mat CDR.** CDR offline phai duoc luu tru dang tin cay. Mat CDR = mat doanh thu. Dung luu tru ben vung.

5. **Sai che do tinh cuoc.** Ap dung online cho tra sau (khong can thiet) hoac offline cho tra truoc (khong kiem soat) gay van de.

6. **Vong lap redirect.** Neu trang redirect tinh vao quota, nguoi dung khong the nap tien. Whitelist URL nap tien.

7. **Phuc tap roaming.** V-CHF vs H-CHF phu thuoc thoa thuan roaming. Dam bao dinh tuyen dung.

8. **Cap session vs cap flow.** Cung session co the co flow voi che do tinh cuoc khac nhau. Theo doi rieng.

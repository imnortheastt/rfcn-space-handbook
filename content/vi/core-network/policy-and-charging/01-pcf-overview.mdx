<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

PCF la bo nao quyet dinh moi nguoi dung co the lam gi tren mang. Nguoi dung nay co the bat dau goi video khong? Ho nen nhan QoS gi? Ho da vuot gioi han du lieu chua?

Khong co PCF, moi thue bao se duoc xu ly giong nhau — khong phan biet, khong thuc thi, khong mo hinh kinh doanh.

</section>

## Truc giac


## Ap dung

**Cac tinh huong policy pho bien:**

| Tinh huong | Hanh dong PCF | Hieu ung SMF |
|------------|---------------|--------------|
| Thue bao premium | AMBR cao hon, 5QI uu tien | QoS flow tot hon |
| Dat gioi han du lieu | AMBR = 1 Mbps | Throttling tai UPF |
| Bat dau goi VoLTE | Them quy tac GBR | Bearer thoai rieng |
| Roaming | Ap dung policy PLMN visited | Dich vu han che |
| Enterprise slice | Quy tac dac thu slice | QoS rieng |

**Debug PCF:**

```bash
# Wireshark: hoat dong dich vu PCF
http2 && sbi && pcf

# N7 tao policy
http2.header.path contains "sm-policies"

# N5 uy quyen AF
http2.header.path contains "app-sessions"

# Kiem tra quyet dinh policy
grep "PccRule" /var/log/pcf/*.log
```

**Cac hoat dong API chinh:**

| Giao dien | Hoat dong | Muc dich |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| N7 (SMF) | SMPoliciesPost | Tao SM policy |
| N7 (SMF) | SMPoliciesUpdate | Sua doi policy |
| N15 (AMF) | AMPoliciesPost | Tao AM policy |
| N5 (AF) | PolicyAuthorizationCreate | AF yeu cau QoS |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Thieu lien ket policy.** SMF phai tao SM policy truoc khi session hoan tat. Khong lien ket = mac dinh/tu choi.

2. **UDR khong kha dung.** PCF khong the lay policy thue bao neu UDR bi loi. Can quy tac du phong.

3. **Thoi gian policy.** Cap nhat PCF co the den trong khi session dang sua doi. Co the xay ra race condition.

4. **Khong khop uy quyen AF.** AF yeu cau QoS ma thue bao khong cho phep. PCF phai tu choi hoac giam cap.

5. **Policy cu.** Quyet dinh policy dua tren du lieu cu (thue bao thay doi, vi tri thay doi). Dang ky su kien.

6. **Xung dot policy slice.** Cac slice khac nhau co the co quy tac policy khac nhau. Dam bao context slice dung.

7. **Tich hop tinh cuoc.** PCF dua vao CHF cho su kien su dung. Thieu tich hop = khong co policy dua tren su dung.

8. **Local breakout.** Tinh huong roaming can tuong tac V-PCF/H-PCF. Quyen policy phu thuoc thoa thuan roaming.

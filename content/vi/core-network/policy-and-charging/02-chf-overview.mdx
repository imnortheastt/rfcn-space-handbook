## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

CHF la cach nha mang duoc tra tien. No theo doi moi byte, moi phut, moi dich vu su dung — sau do cung cap du lieu do cho he thong billing.

Khong co CHF, khong co cach tinh cuoc nguoi dung tra truoc theo thoi gian thuc hoac tao hoa don tra sau chinh xac.

</section>

## Truc giac

### CHF lam gi

```
Cac Chuc nang CHF:

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                          │
│  Theo doi Su dung:                                      │
│  ├── Luong du lieu (byte UL/DL)                        │
│  ├── Thoi luong (thoi gian session)                    │
│  ├── Su kien (SMS gui, cuoc goi thuc hien)             │
│  └── Dac thu dich vu (phut video, MB roaming)          │
│                                                          │
│  Kiem soat Tinh cuoc:                                   │
│  ├── Cap quota (tra truoc)                             │
│  ├── Bao cao su dung (tra sau)                         │
│  ├── Kich hoat thay doi policy (gioi han du lieu)      │
│  └── Tu choi dich vu (het credit)                      │
│                                                          │
│  Tao CDR:                                               │
│  ├── Tao Charging Data Records                         │
│  ├── Chuyen tiep den billing/mediation                 │
│  └── Ho tro xu ly offline                              │
│                                                          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Ap dung

**Cac tinh huong tinh cuoc pho bien:**

| Tinh huong | Hanh vi CHF | Ket qua |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| Goi video tra truoc | Cap quota thoai, theo doi phut | Tru tu so du |
| Roaming tra sau | Tich luy CDR roaming | Tinh cuoi thang |
| Kich hoat gioi han du lieu | Thong bao PCF tai nguong | Policy throttle toc do |
| Zero-rating | Khong tinh noi dung sponsor | Theo doi su dung, khong tinh tien |
| Slice doanh nghiep | Ap dung gia doanh nghiep | Gia khac nhau |

**Debug tinh cuoc:**

```bash
# Wireshark: hoat dong dich vu CHF
http2.header.path contains "nchf-convergedcharging"

# Tao/cap nhat/giai phong tinh cuoc
http2.header.method == "POST" && http2.header.path contains "chargingData"

# Kiem tra quota duoc cap
grep "grantedUnit" /var/log/smf/*.log

# Dau ra CDR
ls -la /var/spool/chf/cdr/
```

**Cac hoat dong API chinh:**

| Hoat dong | Muc dich |
|-----------|----------|
| ChargingDataCreate | Bat dau session tinh cuoc |
| ChargingDataUpdate | Bao cao su dung, lay them quota |
| ChargingDataRelease | Ket thuc session, su dung cuoi |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **CHF khong kha dung.** Neu CHF bi loi, nguoi dung tra truoc khong the ket noi. Can policy du phong (quota gioi han, chi khan cap).

2. **Race condition het quota.** Nguoi dung co the vuot quota giua cap nhat va thuc thi. Cho phep dung sai nho.

3. **Loi rating.** Ap dung sai bieu gia = tranh chap billing. Xac nhan quy tac rating ky luong.

4. **Mat CDR.** CDR phai duoc luu tru dang tin cay. Mat chung = mat doanh thu. Dung luu tru ben vung, sao chep.

5. **Tinh cuoc roaming.** Tinh cuoc nha vs visited phu thuoc thoa thuan roaming. Can mediation phuc tap.

6. **Tinh cuoc multi-slice.** Cac slice khac nhau co the co gia khac nhau. Dam bao context slice trong yeu cau tinh cuoc.

7. **Phuc tap tinh cuoc hoi tu.** Ket hop online+offline trong mot chuc nang tang do phuc tap. Can phan tach logic ro rang.

8. **Anh huong do tre.** Tinh cuoc online them do tre vao thiet lap session. Toi uu hieu suat CHF.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Tram goc truyen thong dong goi moi thu: RF, baseband, dieu khien, va backhaul. Phan tach cho phep linh hoat — chay mot so phan tap trung, mot so tai edge, ket hop cac vendor.

Nhung phan tach o dau? Moi tuy chon danh doi giua bang thong fronthaul, dung sai do tre, va loi gop chung. Nganh cong nghiep hoi tu vao Option 7-2x cho O-RAN, nhung de hieu tai sao can biet cac lua chon khac.

</section>

## Ap dung

**Lua chon tuy chon phan tach:**

| Truong hop | Split Khuyen nghi | Ly do |
|------------|-------------------|-------|
| Macro cell | Option 2 + 7-2x | Gop CU + O-RAN |
| Small cell | Option 2 + 7-2x | Cung he sinh thai |
| Massive MIMO | 7-2x Cat-B | Hieu qua fronthaul |
| Nong thon (transport han che) | Chi Option 2 | Giam BW FH |
| Cloud RAN | 7-2x | Gop chung toi da |

**Tinh kich co fronthaul:**

```bash
# Tinh fronthaul cho 100 MHz TDD, 64T64R, Cat-B
# Bit IQ: 2 (I+Q) × 9 bits × 12 subcarriers × 273 PRBs × 14 symbols
# Moi ms: ~7.5 Gbps moi huong (truoc nen)
# Voi BFP: ~4 Gbps moi huong
# Hai huong: ~8-10 Gbps dien hinh, 25 Gbps dinh

# Transport khuyen nghi:
# - 25G Ethernet cho don sector
# - 100G Ethernet cho site gop chung
```

**Tom tat interface:**

| Interface | Giao thuc | Vi tri |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| F1 | GTP-U/SCTP | CU ↔ DU |
| Open Fronthaul | eCPRI | DU ↔ RU |
| E1 | SCTP | CU-CP ↔ CU-UP |
| E2 | SCTP | Near-RT RIC ↔ CU/DU |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Danh gia thap fronthaul.** 64T64R can 25G+ ngay ca khi nen. Lap ke hoach dung luong transport.

2. **Qua ngan sach do tre.** Option 7-2x can RTT < 250 µs. Do dai cap quang va hop switch cong lai.

3. **Tron cac split.** Cac vendor khac co the trien khai 7-2x hoi khac. Xac minh tuong thich.

4. **Bo qua timing.** Tat ca split can dong bo chinh xac. Option 7-2x can pha ±1.5 µs cho TDD.

5. **Khong khop Cat-A/B.** DU phai khop loai RU. Ghep sai pha vo beamforming.

6. **Loi nen.** Nen IQ manh lam giam EVM. Can bang bang thong vs chat luong.

7. **Khoa CPRI.** Option 8 (CPRI) rieng moi vendor. O-RAN 7-2x la lua chon mo.

8. **Do phuc tap nang cap.** Doi split can phan cung moi ca hai dau. Lap ke hoach cho vong doi.

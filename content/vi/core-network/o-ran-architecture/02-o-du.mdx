## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

O-DU la noi cac quyet dinh mili giay xay ra. Moi 0.5ms, no quyet dinh UE nao duoc khoi tai nguyen nao. Moi 1ms, no xu ly truyen lai HARQ.

Hieu O-DU la can thiet vi day la noi hieu nang 5G duoc tao ra hoac pha vo — do tre, throughput, va hieu qua pho deu phu thuoc vao nhung gi xay ra o day.

</section>

## Ap dung

**Can nhac trien khai O-DU:**

| Yeu to | Edge (gan RU) | Tap trung |
|--------|---------------|-----------|
| Do tre fronthaul | < 100 µs | < 250 µs |
| Bang thong fronthaul | Thap hon | Cao hon (gop) |
| Gop chung compute | Khong | Chia se giua cell |
| Ban kinh loi | Mot cell | Nhieu cell |

**Debug O-DU:**

```bash
# Theo doi scheduler
grep "sched_decision" /var/log/du/mac.log

# Thong ke HARQ
mac-cli show harq stats

# Trang thai buffer RLC
rlc-cli show buffer-status --ue-id 1234

# Thoi gian fronthaul
fh-cli show timing-advance

# Kiem tra su dung PRB
mac-cli show prb-utilization --interval 1s
```

**Chi so hieu nang chinh:**

| Chi so | Muc tieu | Nghiem trong |
|--------|----------|--------------|
| Chu ky scheduler | < 100 µs | > 200 µs |
| RTT HARQ | 4-8 ms | > 16 ms |
| Ty le truyen lai RLC | < 1% | > 5% |
| Su dung PRB | 60-80% | > 95% |
| Jitter fronthaul | < 10 µs | > 50 µs |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Lo deadline scheduler.** Neu scheduler khong the quyet dinh kip thoi, slot bi lang phi. Dung thuat toan xac dinh.

2. **Tran buffer HARQ.** Qua nhieu tien trinh HARQ dang cho can kiet bo nho. Giam sat su dung HARQ.

3. **Timeout lap ghep RLC.** Segment bi thieu timeout va kich hoat truyen lai. Dat timer phu hop.

4. **Bien thien tre fronthaul.** Jitter gay den muon. O-RU bo goi den muon.

5. **Phan manh PRB.** Lap lich kem de lai khoang trong trong cap phat PRB. Giam hieu qua pho.

6. **CQI cu.** Su dung CQI cu dan den chon MCS sai. Dam bao phan hoi kenh moi.

7. **Chuyen doi UL/DL.** Chuyen doi TDD them phuc tap. Khoang bao ve phai duoc ton trong.

8. **Phoi hop MIMO da nguoi dung.** MU-MIMO yeu cau lap lich phoi hop. Ghep cap UE sai lang phi dung luong.

<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

O-RU la phan duy nhat cua RAN thuc su cham vao RF. No chuyen doi mau IQ so thanh song vo tuyen va nguoc lai. Moi thu khac la xu ly so.

Trong O-RAN, RU tro nen co the thay the duoc. Hieu cac kha nang va rang buoc cua O-RU la can thiet cho trien khai da vendor va lap ke hoach tram.

</section>

## Ap dung

**Tieu chi chon O-RU:**

| Yeu to | Small cell | Macro (4T4R) | Massive MIMO |
|--------|------------|--------------|--------------|
| Category | A | A hoac B | B |
| Cong suat | 1-5W | 20-80W | 200W+ |
| Antenna | 2T2R | 4T4R | 64T64R |
| Fronthaul | 10G | 25G | 25G-100G |
| Trong luong | < 5 kg | 10-20 kg | 30-50 kg |

**Debug O-RU:**

```bash
# Kiem tra lien ket fronthaul
ethtool eth0 | grep "Speed\|Link"

# Trang thai dong bo PTP
pmc -u -b 0 'GET TIME_STATUS_NP'

# Canh bao O-RU qua NETCONF
netconf-cli get --filter "<alarms/>"

# Thong ke eCPRI
ecpri-cli show stats

# Kiem tra VSWR
oru-cli show vswr --port all
```

**Tham so O-RU pho bien:**

| Tham so | Pham vi Dien hinh | Ghi chu |
|---------|-------------------|---------|
| Cong suat TX | 2-200W moi port | Class dinh nghia max |
| Bang tan | 600 MHz - 40 GHz | Theo model O-RU |
| Bang thong | 5-100 MHz | Moi song mang |
| SCS | 15, 30, 60, 120 kHz | Phu thuoc bang |
| EVM | < 8% (256QAM) | Chi so chat luong RF |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Mat dong bo thoi gian.** Neu PTP that bai, O-RU TDD phai ngung truyen. Dam bao nguon thoi gian du phong.

2. **Canh bao VSWR.** VSWR cao chi ra van de antenna/feeder. Co the lam hong PA neu bo qua.

3. **Giam cong suat do nhiet.** O-RU giam cong suat khi nhiet do cao. Lam mat tram la quan trong.

4. **Jitter fronthaul.** Goi den muon bi bo. Dung dinh hinh luu luong va hang doi uu tien.

5. **Loi nen IQ.** Nen qua manh lam giam EVM. Giam sat hieu nang ria cell.

6. **Khong khop Cat-A/B.** O-DU phai biet loai O-RU. Gia dinh sai pha vo beamforming.

7. **Thoi gian gop song mang.** Nhieu song mang can thang hang pha chat. Xac minh dong bo tung song mang.

8. **Khong tuong thich firmware.** Spec O-RAN phat trien. Phien ban firmware O-DU va O-RU phai tuong thich.

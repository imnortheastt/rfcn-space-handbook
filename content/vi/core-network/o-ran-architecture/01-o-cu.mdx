## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Trong RAN truyen thong, tram goc lam moi thu — RF, xu ly tin hieu, lap lich, RRC. O-RAN chia thanh cac thanh phan co the den tu cac vendor khac nhau.

O-CU xu ly cac "lop tren" — RRC cho control plane, PDCP cho user plane. Hieu O-CU la can thiet cho trien khai RAN da vendor.

</section>

## Ap dung

**Cac phuong an trien khai O-CU:**

| Phuong an | CU-CP | CU-UP | Truong hop Su dung |
|-----------|-------|-------|-------------------|
| Tich hop | Cung node | Cung node | Trien khai don gian |
| Tach biet | Tap trung | Edge | Do tre UP thap |
| Gop chung | Chia se | Tung edge | Hieu qua control plane |
| Du phong | Active-standby | Can bang tai | HA cao |

**Debug O-CU:**

```bash
# Wireshark: tin nhan F1AP
f1ap

# Tin nhan E1AP
e1ap

# Kiem tra F1 setup
grep "F1SetupRequest\|F1SetupResponse" /var/log/cu-cp/*.log

# Thao tac bearer context
grep "BearerContext" /var/log/cu-cp/*.log

# Thong ke PDCP
pdcp-cli show stats --drb
```

**Chi so chinh:**

| Chi so | Dien hinh | Canh bao |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Do tre F1 (CP) | < 10 ms | > 50 ms |
| Do tre F1 (UP) | < 5 ms | > 20 ms |
| Do tre E1 | < 5 ms | > 20 ms |
| Thoi gian RRC setup | < 200 ms | > 500 ms |
| Throughput PDCP | Toc do line | Co drop |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Do tin cay truyen tai F1.** F1 dung SCTP (CP) va UDP (UP). Van de mang giua CU va DU anh huong truc tiep den dich vu.

2. **Do phuc tap tach E1.** Tach CU-CP va CU-UP them do tre. Giu lien ket E1 ngan.

3. **Dong bo UE context.** CU-CP, CU-UP, va DU phai co UE context nhat quan. Loi dong bo gay drop.

4. **Buffer sap xep lai PDCP.** CU-UP dem goi khong theo thu tu. Tran buffer gay mat goi.

5. **Chuyen tiep handover.** Khi handover, CU-UP nguon chuyen tiep den dich. Duong chuyen tiep phai nhanh.

6. **Mat ROHC context.** Context nen header phai duoc duy tri. Khong khop context gay loi giai nen.

7. **Phoi hop da DU.** Mot CU-CP co the phuc vu nhieu DU. Overhead quan ly va F1 setup tang.

8. **Xu ly khoa bao mat.** CU-CP suy dien va phan phoi khoa. Khong khop khoa pha vo ma hoa.

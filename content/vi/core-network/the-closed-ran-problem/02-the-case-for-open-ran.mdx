## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Open RAN hua hen pha vo khoa nha cung cap thong qua giao dien chuan hoa, phan tach phan cung-phan mem, va tuong thich da nha cung cap.

Hieu cac loi ich nay giup danh gia xem Open RAN co phu hop voi mang cua ban khong.

## Ap dung

**Danh gia muc do san sang Open RAN:**

| Yeu to | Cau hoi | San sang neu... |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| Greenfield vs Brownfield | Xay moi hay hien co? | Greenfield = de hon |
| Loai phu song | Do thi day hay nong thon? | Nong thon = phu hop |
| Truong hop su dung | eMBB hay URLLC? | eMBB da chin muoi |
| Ky nang | Co doi cloud-native? | Co = ap dung nhanh hon |
| Thoi gian | Can san xuat &lt;6 thang? | Thoi gian dai = an toan hon |

**Khung quyet dinh trien khai:**

```bash
# Khi Open RAN hop ly:

1. Mang greenfield
   - Khong can tich hop legacy
   - Kien truc sach
   - Vi du: nguoi moi vao thi truong

2. Mo rong nong thon
   - Yeu cau hieu nang thap hon
   - Do nhay cam chi phi cao hon
   - Moi truong RF it phuc tap

3. Truong hop su dung cu the
   - Mang rieng doanh nghiep
   - Phu song trong nha (thay the DAS)
   - Trien khai neutral host

4. Da dang hoa chien luoc
   - Giam tap trung nha cung cap
   - Xay dung chuyen mon noi bo
   - Chuan bi cho lam moi tuong lai

# Khi can than trong:

1. Tram macro do thi dung luong
   - Hieu nang quan trong
   - Phuc tap tich hop
   - Nha cung cap da chung minh co the thang

2. Thoi gian chat
   - Tich hop da nha cung cap mat nhieu thoi gian hon
   - He sinh thai van dang chin muoi
   - Rui ro cham tre

3. Chuyen mon noi bo han che
   - Can ky nang cloud-native
   - Quan ly da nha cung cap
   - Kha nang tich hop
```

</section>

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **"Mo" вү  cam la chay.** Giao dien mo ton tai nhung van can thu nghiem tich hop. Ngan sach cho xac thuc tuong thich.

2. **Khoang cach hieu nang.** Giai phap Open RAN som co khoang cach 10-15% so voi truyen thong. Dang thu hep nhung can xac minh.

3. **Do chin he sinh thai.** Khong phai tat ca thanh phan deu chin muoi nhu nhau. Lua chon RU it hon truyen thong. Xac minh tinh kha dung.

4. **Phuc tap tich hop.** Da nha cung cap = nhieu no luc tich hop hon. Can doi ky nang hoac nha tich hop he thong.

5. **Chi phi an.** Phan cung COTS + giay phep phan mem + tich hop co the vuot truyen thong trong mot so truong hop. Can phan tich TCO day du.

6. **Mo hinh ho tro.** Da nha cung cap = rui ro do loi cho nhau. Lam ro trach nhiem ho tro truoc.

7. **Tieu thu dien.** Trien khai vRAN som tieu thu nhieu dien hon ASIC. Dang cai thien nhung can xac minh.

8. **Bang thong fronthaul.** Chia tach Option 7.2x can dung luong fronthaul dang ke. Thiet ke mang quan trong.

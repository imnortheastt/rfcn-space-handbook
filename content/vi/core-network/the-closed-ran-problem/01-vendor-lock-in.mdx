<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Trien khai RAN truyen thong ràng buoc nha mang voi mot nha cung cap trong 7-10 nam. Viec khoa nay lam tang chi phi, cham doi moi, va han che linh hoat trien khai.

Hieu khoa nha cung cap la buoc dau tien de danh gia cac lua chon thay the Open RAN.

</section>

## Ap dung

**Danh sach kiem tra danh gia khoa:**

| Cau hoi | Co den Do |
|---------|-----------|
| Co the ket hop RRU tu hang A voi BBU tu hang B? | Khong |
| Dinh dang config/PM duoc cong bo cong khai? | Khong |
| Co the chay phan mem tren phan cung thuong mai? | Khong |
| Hop dong cho phep tich hop ben thu 3? | Han che |
| Kich hoat tinh nang tach biet mua HW? | Gop chung |
| Co the chuyen dau tu sang nen tang the he moi? | Khong |

**Diem don bay dam phan:**

```bash
# Cau hoi can hoi nha cung cap khi RFP:

1. Tai lieu giao dien
   "Cung cap spec giao dien CPRI/fronthaul day du"
   → Neu tu choi, cho thay y dinh khoa doc quyen

2. Di dong du lieu
   "Xuat tat ca du lieu PM/CM theo dinh dang chuan"
   → Neu khong chuan, se ton chi phi di chuyen

3. Linh hoat phan mem
   "Chay stack cua ban tren phan cung COTS chung toi chon"
   → Neu tu choi, khoa phan cung

4. Dieu khoan da nha cung cap
   "Tich hop voi EMS cua doi thu qua API mo"
   → Khang cu cho thay he sinh thai dong

5. Dieu khoan thoat ra
   "Du lieu/configs nao chung toi giu lai neu chuyen?"
   → Tra loi mo ho = bi mac ket
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Tuyen bo "mo".** Nha cung cap tuyen bo "mo" nhung chi trong he sinh thai cua ho. Yeu cau bang chung tuong thich ben thu 3.

2. **Cap phep tinh nang leo thang.** HW co ban re, nhung moi tinh nang (CA, MIMO, beamforming) ban rieng.

3. **Phu thuoc ho tro.** Khong the xu ly su co khong co nha cung cap do binary dong. Ky nang doi van hanh mai mot.

4. **Tu dong gia han hop dong.** Hop dong nhieu nam tu dong gia han. Bo lo cua so, bi khoa them mot ky han.

5. **Khoa dao tao.** Nhan vien chi duoc dao tao tren he thong mot nha cung cap. Kien thuc to chuc tro thanh rieng nha cung cap.

6. **No tich hop.** Nhieu nam adapter OSS tuy chinh cho mot nha cung cap. Chuyen doi nghia la viet lai moi thu.

7. **Nguy bien chi phi chim.** "Chung toi da dau tu qua nhieu de chuyen." Tiep tuc tra gia cao vo thoi han.

8. **Can chinh lo trinh.** Thoi gian 5G cua nha cung cap khong khop ngay ra mat cua nha mang. Khong co lua chon.

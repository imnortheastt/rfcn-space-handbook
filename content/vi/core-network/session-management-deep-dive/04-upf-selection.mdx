<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Khi UE yeu cau PDU session, SMF phai chon UPF nao xu ly luu luong. Chon sai = do tre cao, dinh tuyen khong toi uu, hoac khong the ap dung policy.

Trong edge computing va 5G slicing, lua chon UPF tro nen quan trong — UPF quyet dinh noi luu luong vao mang du lieu.

</section>

## Ap dung

**Tieu chi lua chon theo kich ban:**

| Kich ban | Tieu chi Chinh | Che do SSC |
|----------|----------------|------------|
| Internet co ban | DNN, slice, tai | SSC 1 |
| Gaming edge | Vi tri, do tre | SSC 2 hoac 3 |
| VPN doanh nghiep | DNN, yeu cau neo | SSC 1 |
| LADN nha may | Vung dich vu, UPF cuc bo | SSC 2 |
| Streaming video | Dung luong, DNN | SSC 1 hoac 2 |

**Debug lua chon UPF:**

```bash
# Kiem tra NRF cho UPF da dang ky
curl -X GET "http://nrf:8080/nnrf-disc/v1/nf-instances?target-nf-type=UPF"

# Log SMF cho quyet dinh lua chon
grep "UPF selection" /var/log/smf/*.log

# Xac minh N4 session den UPF dung
tcpdump -i any port 8805 -w pfcp.pcap

# Trang thai PFCP association
pfcp_client status
```

**Cac xem xet cau hinh:**

| Cau hinh | Anh huong |
|----------|-----------|
| Vung dich vu UPF | Gioi han noi UPF co the duoc chon |
| Anh xa DNN-den-UPF | UPF nao phuc vu DNN nao |
| S-NSSAI-den-UPF | Pool UPF danh rieng cho slice |
| Cau hinh LADN | Ranh gioi dich vu dia ly |
| Nguong tai | Khi nao tranh UPF qua tai |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Khong co UPF cho slice.** Neu S-NSSAI yeu cau khong co UPF dang ky, thiet lap session that bai. Xac minh cau hinh slice.

2. **DNN khong duoc phuc vu.** UPF phai co giao dien den DNN yeu cau. Thieu giao dien N6 = session bi tu choi.

3. **Khong khop che do SSC.** UE yeu cau SSC 2 nhung thue bao chi cho phep SSC 1 → dam phan hoac tu choi.

4. **LADN ngoai vung.** UE yeu cau LADN DNN nhung o ngoai vung dich vu → thiet lap bi tu choi.

5. **Chon UPF qua tai.** Khong co can bang tai dung cach, SMF co the chon UPF tac nghen. Su dung thong tin tai NRF.

6. **Edge UPF khong the lien lac.** Neu edge UPF bi loi, can fallback ve UPF trung tam. Lap ke hoach du phong.

7. **Cau hinh sai UL classifier.** Bo loc goi sai gui luu luong den UPF sai. Kiem tra quy tac dinh tuyen.

8. **Du lieu NRF cu.** Cache NRF co the co thong tin UPF loi thoi. Dam bao heartbeat/subscription hoat dong.

<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

PDU session khong ton tai mai mai. Khi ban dong ung dung, het du lieu, hoac bi huy dang ky, session phai duoc **giai phong**. Dieu nay giai phong dia chi IP, tai nguyen UPF, va bearer RAN.

Giai phong dung cach la thiet yeu — session bi ro ri tieu thu tai nguyen mang vo thoi han.

</section>

<section class="lesson-apply">

## Ap dung

**Tinh huong giai phong pho bien:**

| Tinh huong | Nguoi khoi tao | Nguyen nhan |
|------------|----------------|-------------|
| Nguoi dung bat che do may bay | UE | huy dang ky switch-off |
| Vuot gioi han du lieu | Mang (PCF) | vi pham policy |
| Het so du tra truoc | Mang (CHF) | su kien tinh cuoc |
| Timeout khong hoat dong | Mang (SMF) | timer het han |
| Bao tri mang | Mang (OAM) | hanh dong admin |

**Debug giai phong:**

```bash
# Bo loc Wireshark cho tin nhan release
nas-5gs.sm.message_type == 0xd1 || nas-5gs.sm.message_type == 0xd3 || nas-5gs.sm.message_type == 0xd4

# N4 session deletion
pfcp.message_type == 5 || pfcp.message_type == 6

# Kiem tra session bi bo roi
grep "session.state" /var/log/smf/*.log | grep -v "released"
```

**Xu ly su co:**

| Van de | Kiem tra |
|--------|----------|
| Session khong giai phong | SMF bi ket? N4 timeout? |
| IP khong tra ve pool | UPF deletion response nhan duoc? |
| DRB khong giai phong | Tin nhan N2 den gNB da gui? |
| Su dung khong bao cao | URR da cau hinh? Bao cao cuoi nhan duoc? |

</section>

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Quen xoa N4.** SMF phai xoa PFCP session. Thieu dieu nay ro ri tai nguyen UPF.

2. **Khong bao cao su dung cuoi.** Neu URR da cau hinh, UPF nen gui bao cao su dung cuoi. Thieu dieu nay gay loi tinh cuoc.

3. **Tai nguyen RAN khong giai phong.** AMF phai gui N2 release den gNB. Thieu dieu nay giu DRB da cap phat.

4. **Can pool IP.** Neu session khong giai phong dung cach, pool IP can. Giam sat muc su dung pool.

5. **Khong giai phong lien ket PCF.** SMF nen thong bao PCF. Lien ket policy cu lang phi tai nguyen PCF.

6. **Bo qua nguyen nhan release.** Cac nguyen nhan khac nhau can xu ly khac nhau. "Reactivation requested" nghia la UE nen thu lai.

7. **Giai phong trong handover.** Dung giai phong session chi vi gNB thay doi. Chi chuyen duong, session van con.

8. **Session multi-homed.** UE voi ca 3GPP va non-3GPP access — giai phong mot khong giai phong cai kia.

<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Khi PDU session da thiet lap, no khong giu nguyen. Ban bat dau goi video — mang them QoS flow. Ban dat gioi han du lieu — mang giam AMBR. Ban di chuyen den cell edge — UPF thay doi.

**Sua doi session** xu ly tat ca cac thay doi runtime nay ma khong can huy va xay dung lai session.

</section>

| **AF** | App ben ngoai yeu cau QoS qua NEF |

<section class="lesson-apply">

## Ap dung

**Trigger sua doi pho bien:**

| Trigger | Thay doi gi |
|---------|-------------|
| Bat dau goi video | Them QoS flow GBR (5QI=2) |
| Ket thuc goi video | Xoa QoS flow GBR |
| Dat gioi han du lieu | Giam session AMBR |
| Handover | Cap nhat endpoint tunnel N3 |
| Edge breakout | Them quy tac UL classifier |
| Che do gaming | Dieu chinh tham so QoS |

**Debug sua doi:**

```bash
# Bo loc Wireshark cho tin nhan modification
nas-5gs.sm.message_type == 0xc5 || nas-5gs.sm.message_type == 0xca || nas-5gs.sm.message_type == 0xcb

# N4 session modification
pfcp.message_type == 3 || pfcp.message_type == 4
```

**Loai tin nhan chinh:**

| Tin nhan | Huong | Muc dich |
|----------|-------|----------|
| PDU Session Modification Request | UE→Mang | UE muon thay doi |
| PDU Session Modification Command | Mang→UE | Mang thay doi session |
| PDU Session Modification Complete | UE→Mang | UE xac nhan lenh |
| PDU Session Modification Reject | Mang→UE | Thay doi bi tu choi |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Bo qua lenh modification.** Khi mang gui lenh modification, UE phai ap dung. Bo qua co the gay giai phong session.

2. **QoS flow khong co bo loc goi.** Them QoS flow yeu cau bo loc goi de phan loai luu luong. Thieu bo loc nghia la luu luong dung flow mac dinh.

3. **Yeu cau GBR khong co tai nguyen.** Yeu cau bitrate dam bao khi mang tac nghen → modification bi tu choi.

4. **N4 cap nhat that bai.** Neu SMF khong the cap nhat UPF, modification that bai. Kiem tra ket noi N4.

5. **Quen cap nhat RAN.** Mot so modification can cau hinh lai gNB (DRB moi). AMF phai gui tin nhan N2.

6. **Van de thoi gian policy.** Thay doi policy PCF co the khong den SMF ngay. Policy cu co the xung dot voi modification.

7. **Khong nhat quan multi-access.** UE tren ca 3GPP va non-3GPP phai co quy tac QoS nhat quan tren ca hai access.

8. **Session AMBR vs UE AMBR.** Dung nham session AMBR (moi PDU session) voi UE AMBR (tren tat ca session).

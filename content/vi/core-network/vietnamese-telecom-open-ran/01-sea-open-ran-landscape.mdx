## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Gia dinh dong nhat.** Dong Nam A da dang. Singapore ≠ Indonesia ≠ Viet Nam ve do chin va cach tiep can.

2. **Bo qua dia chinh tri.** Lua chon nha cung cap mang tinh chinh tri. Dong luc My-Trung anh huong quyet dinh.

3. **Danh gia thap ha tang.** Thieu dien va backhaul anh huong gia dinh TCO Open RAN.

4. **Phong dai lenh chinh sach.** Hau het chinh phu Dong Nam A khuyen khich, khong bat buoc, Open RAN.

5. **Thieu boi canh dia phuong.** Quan he nha mang, phan bo pho tan, va dong luc canh tranh khac nhau.

6. **Mu quang lo hong ky nang.** Open RAN can ky nang khac RAN truyen thong. Dong Nam A co lo hong.

7. **Gia dinh kinh te quy mo.** Thi truong Dong Nam A thay doi tu nho (Brunei) den khong lo (Indonesia).

8. **Lac quan ve lo trinh.** Ap dung Open RAN tai Dong Nam A tinh bang nam, khong phai quy.

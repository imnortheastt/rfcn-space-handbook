## Ap dung

**Danh gia tuyen bo tuong thich nha cung cap:**

| Tuyen bo | Xac minh |
|----------|----------|
| "O-RAN Compliant" | Tu khai bao, khong kiem thu. Yeu cau chung nhan OTIC. |
| "Da kiem thu tai PlugFest" | Kiem tra bao cao O-RAN TIFG. Giao dien nao? Ket qua gi? |
| "O-RAN Certified" | Xac minh tren danh sach san pham certified O-RAN Alliance |
| "Da kiem thu tuong thich voi X" | Yeu cau bao cao kiem thu. Song phuong hay nha mang chung kien? |
| "Da trien khai san xuat" | Khach hang tham chieu. Truong hop su dung tuong tu? |

**Yeu cau tuong thich RFP:**

```bash
# Yeu cau bat buoc cho RFP:

1. Chung nhan
   "San pham phai O-RAN Certified (cap Silver hoac Gold)
    cho tat ca giao dien duoc mua sam"

2. Tham gia PlugFest
   "NCC phai chung minh tham gia it nhat
    2 PlugFest Toan cau O-RAN trong 18 thang qua"

3. Bang chung tuong thich
   "Cung cap bao cao kiem thu cho thay tuong thich voi
    it nhat 3 san pham NCC khac moi giao dien"

4. Cam ket lab nha mang
   "NCC dong y tham gia kiem thu lab tich hop nha mang
    toi thieu 4 tuan"

5. Ho tro thu nghiem thuc dia
   "NCC cung cap ho tro ky thuat tai cho trong
    giai doan thu nghiem thuc dia (toi thieu 3 thang)"

6. Kiem thu hoi quy
   "NCC cam ket kiem thu hoi quy cho tat ca
    cap nhat phan mem truoc khi trien khai"
```

**Cau hoi chinh cho nha cung cap:**

```bash
# Trong qua trinh danh gia NCC:

Q1: "Cac giao dien cu the nao duoc chung nhan?"
    → Chi Fronthaul? E2? O1? Tat ca?

Q2: "Test cases nao dat/khong dat tai PlugFest gan nhat?"
    → Yeu cau ket qua chi tiet, khong chi tham gia

Q3: "Phien ban firmware/software nao duoc chung nhan?"
    → Phien ban hien tai hay cu hon?

Q4: "Ban da tich hop voi NCC nao khac?"
    → Trong lab? Trong san xuat? Cau hinh giong nhau?

Q5: "Quy trinh kiem thu hoi quy cua ban la gi?"
    → Lam the nao dam bao cap nhat khong pha vo tuong thich?

Q6: "Ban co tham gia debug chung khong?"
    → Cam ket hop dong ho tro da nha cung cap?
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **PlugFest ≠ San xuat.** Dieu kien lab khac voi mang thuc. Thanh cong PlugFest can thiet nhung chua du.

2. **Khong khop phien ban.** Phien ban certified co the khac phien ban giao. Xac minh phien ban chinh xac da kiem thu.

3. **Chung nhan mot phan.** NCC certified cho fronthaul nhung khong E2. Kiem tra tung giao dien rieng.

4. **Song phuong vs da phuong.** Da kiem thu voi mot NCC khong co nghia hoat dong voi tat ca. Kiem tra to hop cu the.

5. **Pham vi kiem thu.** Attach co ban da kiem thu nhung khong chuyen giao. Hieu cac kich ban nao da xac thuc.

6. **Phai nhao thoi gian.** Chung nhan tu 2 nam truoc co the khong phan anh san pham hien tai. Kiem tra ngay chung nhan.

7. **Khong khop profile.** Certified tai Profile A nhung ban can tinh nang Profile C. Xac minh profiles kha nang.

8. **Moi truong lab.** PlugFest dung dieu kien ly tuong. Mang thuc co nhieu, bien dong thoi gian.

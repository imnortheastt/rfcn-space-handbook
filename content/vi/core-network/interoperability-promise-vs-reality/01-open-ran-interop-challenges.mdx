## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Open RAN hua hen tuong thich da nha cung cap, nhung "giao dien mo" khong dam bao tich hop lien mach. Hieu cac thach thuc thuc te giup dat ky vong thuc te va lap ke hoach phu hop.

## Ap dung

**Danh sach kiem tra tich hop:**

| Giao dien | Xac minh Chinh | Cong cu/Phuong phap |
|-----------|----------------|---------------------|
| Fronthaul | Can chinh thoi gian | Phan tich giao thuc |
| Fronthaul | Dinh dang mau IQ | Bat va giai ma |
| E2 | Khop mo hinh dich vu | So sanh ASN.1 |
| O1 | Tuong thich YANG | Kiem tra YANG |
| A1 | Schema chinh sach | Kiem tra JSON schema |

**Lenh debug:**

```bash
# Kiem tra thoi gian fronthaul
tcpdump -i eth0 -w fh_capture.pcap port 44944
# Phan tich voi Wireshark O-RAN dissector

# Xac minh dong bo PTP
pmc -u -b 0 'GET TIME_STATUS_NP'
# Kiem tra offset tu master < 100ns

# Trang thai ket noi E2
curl http://e2mgr:3800/v1/nodeb/states
# Tat ca E2 nodes nen hien thi "CONNECTED"

# Kiem thu NETCONF O1
netconf-console --host=odu --port=830 --get-config
# Nen tra ve cau hinh hop le

# Diff mo hinh YANG
pyang --check-update-from=prev.yang current.yang
# Nhan dien thay doi pha vo
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Nham lan "O-RAN Compliant".** Compliant = tu khai bao. Certified = da kiem thu. Yeu cau certification.

2. **Khoa phien ban.** RU firmware v1.2 kiem thu voi DU v3.4. Bat ky cap nhat nao co the pha vo. Kiem thu moi nang cap.

3. **Lab vs thuc dia.** Tich hop lab thanh cong nhung thuc dia co thoi gian, nhieu khac nhau. Ngan sach cho lap lai thuc dia.

4. **Do loi cho nhau.** Da nha cung cap = "khong phai van de cua toi." Gan trach nhiem tich hop trong hop dong.

5. **Chenh lech hieu nang.** Da nha cung cap co the thap hon 5-15% throughput ban dau. Lap ke hoach thoi gian toi uu.

6. **Leo thang ho tro.** NCC A do loi NCC B. Yeu cau debug chung trong hop dong.

7. **Khong khop lo trinh tinh nang.** DU them tinh nang, RU khong ho tro. Phoi hop ke hoach phat hanh.

8. **Mo rong rieng nha mang.** Them tinh nang tuy chinh pha vo tuong thich. Giam thieu mo rong doc quyen.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Dien thoai cua ban khong phat song lien tuc. Khi ban khong dung du lieu, no vao **che do idle** de tiet kiem pin — nhung van dang ky de cuoc goi va tin nhan co the den duoc.

Hieu idle vs connected giai thich tai sao "luon ket noi" khong co nghia la "luon phat song."

</section>

│  │                                                  │    │

│                                                          │

### So sanh trang thai

| Trang thai | Radio | Core | Pin | Do tre |
|------------|-------|------|-----|--------|
| **RRC_IDLE** | Tat | Da dang ky | Tot nhat | Cao (paging) |
| **RRC_INACTIVE** | Tat | Connected | Tot | Trung binh |
| **RRC_CONNECTED** | Bat | Connected | Toi nhat | Tot nhat |

## Ap dung

**Chi bao trang thai:**

| Trang thai | CM | RRC | Hoat dong UE |
|------------|-----|-----|--------------|
| Tat | - | - | Tat nguon |
| Tim kiem | DEREG | IDLE | Tim mang |
| Dang ky idle | IDLE | IDLE | Cho paging |
| Tam dung | CONN | INACTIVE | San sang phuc hoi nhanh |
| Hoat dong | CONN | CONNECTED | Truyen du lieu |

**Timer:**

| Timer | Muc dich | Gia tri thuong gap |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| T3512 | Dang ky dinh ky | 54 phut |
| Paging DRX | Chu ky che do idle | 1.28s |
| Khong hoat dong | CONNECTED→INACTIVE | 2-10s |
| T380 | INACTIVE→IDLE | 10-60s |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Nham lan trang thai CM va RRC.** CM la lop NAS (AMF). RRC la lop AS (gNB). Chung chuyen doi doc lap.

2. **Bo qua RRC_INACTIVE.** Trang thai dac trung 5G nay cung cap tiet kiem do tre lon cho IoT. Khong dung no la lang phi.

3. **Paging DRX qua dai.** Chu ky paging dai tiet kiem pin nhung tang thoi gian thiet lap cuoc goi MT.

4. **C-DRX qua gay gat.** Thoi gian bat DRX rat ngan co the bo lo co hoi lap lich.

5. **Timer INACTIVE khong khop.** Neu T380 het truoc khi du lieu den, UE chuyen sang IDLE va mat loi ich phuc hoi nhanh.

6. **Quen RAN paging.** Trong INACTIVE, RAN paging UE (khong phai CN paging). Khac voi paging che do idle.

7. **TAI list qua nho.** Trong che do idle, UE tu do chon lai cell trong TAI list. Danh sach nho gay TAU thuong xuyen.

8. **Khong cau hinh I-RNTI dung cach.** Tinh duy nhat cua I-RNTI rat quan trong de RRC_INACTIVE hoat dong chinh xac.

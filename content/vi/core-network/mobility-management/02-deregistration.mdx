<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Dang ky dua UE vao mang. **Huy dang ky** dua no ra. Dieu nay xay ra khi ban tat dien thoai, bat che do may bay, hoac mang quyet dinh ngat ket noi ban.

Hieu huy dang ky giup ban debug cac tinh huong "thiet bi bien mat" va toi uu quan ly tai nguyen mang.

</section>

## Truc giac

### Khi nao huy dang ky xay ra

```
Trigger Huy dang ky:

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                          │
│  UE khoi tao:                                           │
│  ├── Tat nguon / shutdown                               │
│  ├── Che do may bay                                     │
│  ├── Rut SIM                                            │
│  └── Tat 5G (thay doi cai dat)                         │
│                                                          │
│  Mang khoi tao:                                         │
│  ├── Thue bao bi cham dut                               │
│  ├── Hanh dong quan tri OAM                             │
│  ├── Xac thuc lai that bai                              │
│  └── Phat hien UE bat hop phap                          │
│                                                          │
│  Ngam dinh (tu dong):                                   │
│  ├── Timer dang ky dinh ky (T3512) het han             │
│  ├── Timer di dong co the lien lac het han             │
│  └── Timer huy dang ky ngam dinh het han               │
│                                                          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Cac loai huy dang ky

| Loai | Nguoi khoi tao | Vi du |
|------|----------------|-------|
| **Ro rang UE** | UE | Nguoi dung tat dien thoai |
| **Ro rang Mang** | AMF | Admin ngat ket noi nguoi dung |
| **Ngam dinh** | Timer | UE im lang qua lau |

## Ap dung

**Cau hinh timer:**

| Timer | Muc dich | Gia tri thuong gap |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| T3512 | Dang ky dinh ky | 54 phut |
| Di dong lien lac | Bat dau paging sau khi lo T3512 | 4 phut |
| Huy dang ky ngam dinh | Giai phong context neu khong lien lac duoc | 4 phut |
| T3522 | Thu lai huy dang ky | 6 giay |

**Debug huy dang ky:**

```bash
# Bo loc Wireshark cho deregistration
nas-5gs.mm.message_type == 0x45 || nas-5gs.mm.message_type == 0x46

# Kiem tra trang thai dang ky UDM
curl -X GET "https://udm.5gc/nudm-uecm/v1/imsi-310260123456789/registrations"
```

**Tinh huong pho bien:**

| Trieu chung | Kiem tra |
|-------------|----------|
| UE khong the ket noi lai sau khi bat lai | Context cu trong AMF, doi huy dang ky ngam dinh |
| Huy dang ky lap di lap lai | Nguyen nhan do mang khoi tao, kiem tra log AMF |
| Session khong duoc giai phong | SMF da duoc thong bao? Kiem tra giao dien N11 |
| UDM van hien thi da dang ky | UECM deregistration that bai, kiem tra SBI |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Quen co switch-off.** Khi UE tat nguon, no dat switch-off=1 de mang khong cho accept. Thieu co nay lang phi thoi gian.

2. **Khong giai phong PDU session.** AMF phai thong bao SMF giai phong session. Thieu dieu nay ro ri tai nguyen.

3. **Bo qua huy dang ky ngam dinh.** Neu UE bien mat (het pin, mat song), huy dang ky ngam dinh la cach don dep duy nhat.

4. **Timer qua gay gat.** Timer huy dang ky ngam dinh rat ngan gay dang ky lai lien tuc cho UE trong vung phu song yeu.

5. **Khong cap nhat UDM.** AMF phai goi Nudm_UECM_Deregistration. Thieu dieu nay de lai ban ghi dang ky cu.

6. **Vong lap dang ky lai.** Neu nguyen nhan huy dang ky yeu cau dang ky lai nhung van de goc khong duoc sua, UE lap vong.

7. **Context khong khop sau loi.** Neu AMF crash giua huy dang ky, trang thai UE va mang lech nhau. Can quy trinh phuc hoi.

8. **Nham lan multi-access.** UE co the dang ky qua 3GPP va non-3GPP cung luc. Huy dang ky phai chi ro access nao.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Truoc khi dien thoai co the goi dien, gui du lieu, hoac lam bat cu dieu gi huu ich tren mang 5G, no phai **dang ky**. Dang ky noi voi mang: "Toi ton tai, xac thuc toi, va theo doi toi o dau."

Thu tuc nay xay ra moi khi ban bat dien thoai, di chuyen den vung moi, hoac dinh ky khi o che do nghi. Hieu dang ky la nen tang de debug van de ket noi.

</section>

### Cac loai dang ky

| Loai | Trigger | Muc dich |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Ban dau** | Bat nguon, lap SIM | Dang ky lan dau hoac moi |
| **Di dong** | Vao RA/TA moi | Cap nhat vi tri voi mang |
| **Dinh ky** | Timer het han | Xac nhan UE van song |
| **Khan cap** | Goi khan cap | Dang ky gioi han khong co SIM |

## Ap dung

**Tham so dang ky pho bien:**

| Tham so | Gia tri | Muc dich |
|---------|---------|----------|
| T3512 | 54 phut | Timer dang ky dinh ky |
| T3502 | 12 giay | Thu lai sau khi reg bi tu choi |
| Kich thuoc TAI list | 16-64 TAI | Giam cap nhat di dong |

**Xu ly su co dang ky:**

| Trieu chung | Nguyen nhan co the | Kiem tra |
|-------------|-------------------|----------|
| Reg tu choi (cause 3) | UE bat hop phap | SIM bi chan/khong hop le |
| Reg tu choi (cause 5) | IMEI khong chap nhan | Thiet bi trong danh sach den |
| Reg tu choi (cause 11) | PLMN khong cho phep | Cau hinh roaming |
| Reg het thoi gian | AUSF/UDM khong the lien lac | Ket noi core |
| Dang ky lap lai | TAI list qua nho | Mo rong TAI list |

**Lenh debug:**

```bash
# Bo loc Wireshark cho NAS registration
nas-5gs.mm.message_type == 0x41 || nas-5gs.mm.message_type == 0x42

# tcpdump cho N2 (NGAP)
tcpdump -i any port 38412 -w registration.pcap
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Nham lan SUCI vs 5G-GUTI.** Dang ky ban dau dung SUCI (SUPI ma hoa). Mobility/periodic dung 5G-GUTI. Gui sai loai ID gay that bai.

2. **TAI list qua nho.** Neu TAI list khong bao phu pattern di chuyen cua UE, cap nhat mobility qua nhieu.

3. **T3512 qua ngan.** Timer dinh ky qua gay lang phi pin UE va tai nguyen mang.

4. **Thieu NSSAI.** Neu UE khong yeu cau slice, slice mac dinh duoc dung. Co the khong phu hop nhu cau dich vu UE.

5. **AMF cu khong the lien lac.** Khi di dong, AMF moi lien he AMF cu de lay context. Neu AMF cu chet, chuyen context that bai.

6. **Timeout xac thuc.** AUSF/UDM phan hoi cham gay timeout dang ky. Giam sat do tre SBI.

7. **SIM/credential khong khop.** K/OPc trong UDR phai khop USIM. Bat ky sai lech = auth that bai = reg tu choi.

8. **Bo qua nguyen nhan tu choi.** Cac nguyen nhan tu choi khac nhau can xu ly khac nhau. Dung xu ly tat ca reject giong nhau.

<section class="lesson-hook">

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Ban dang goi video tren tau. Tau chay 300 km/h, di qua het cell nay den cell khac. Cuoc goi khong bi ngat. Day la **chuyen giao** — mang chuyen ket noi cua ban tu cell nay sang cell khac mot cach lien mach.

Chuyen giao la thu lam cho di dong hoat dong. Khong co no, moi ranh gioi cell se ngat cuoc goi cua ban.

</section>

### Cac loai chuyen giao

| Loai | Giao dien | Khi nao dung |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| **Xn handover** | Xn (gNB-gNB) | Cung AMF, gNB ket noi qua Xn |
| **N2 handover** | N2 (qua AMF) | Khong co Xn, hoac can doi AMF |
| **Inter-RAT** | N26/N2 | Chuyen doi 5G ↔ 4G |

## Ap dung

**Tham so chuyen giao:**

| Tham so | Muc dich | Gia tri thuong gap |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| A3 offset | Neighbor phai tot hon nay | 2-6 dB |
| Time-to-trigger | A3 phai giu bao lau | 40-640 ms |
| Hysteresis | Ngan ping-pong | 1-3 dB |
| T304 | Timer hoan thanh HO | 50-200 ms |

**Xu ly su co chuyen giao:**

| Van de | Nguyen nhan co the | Kiem tra |
|--------|-------------------|----------|
| HO qua muon | A3 offset qua cao | Giam offset |
| HO qua som | A3 offset qua thap | Tang offset |
| Ping-pong HO | Hysteresis qua thap | Tang hysteresis |
| HO that bai | Cell dich bi tac nghen | Admission control |
| Mat du lieu | Chuyen tiep khong hoat dong | Kiem tra Xn/tunnel |

**Bo loc Wireshark:**

```bash
# NGAP handover messages
ngap.procedureCode == 1 || ngap.procedureCode == 0

# Xn handover
xnap.procedureCode == 0 || xnap.procedureCode == 1
```

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Bo qua cau hinh do luong.** UE chi bao cao nhung gi ban yeu cau. Cau hinh sai do luong = khong chuyen giao.

2. **A3 offset qua gay gat.** Offset qua thap gay ping-pong giua cell. Qua cao gay chuyen giao muon va mat cuoc goi.

3. **Khong cau hinh Xn.** Khong co giao dien Xn, moi chuyen giao phai qua AMF (cham hon, nhieu bao hieu hon).

4. **Tat chuyen tiep du lieu.** Trong chuyen giao, du lieu buffer tai gNB nguon nen chuyen tiep den dich. Neu tat, mat goi tin.

5. **Timer T304 qua ngan.** Neu UE khong the hoan thanh chuyen giao truoc khi T304 het, no tuyen bo HO that bai va quay lai nguon.

6. **Khong xu ly HO that bai.** Khi chuyen giao that bai, UE nen quay lai cell nguon. Dam bao re-establishment hoat dong.

7. **Phuc tap Inter-RAT.** Chuyen giao 5G↔4G lien quan den MME/AMF interworking. Phai cau hinh giao dien N26.

8. **Chuyen security context.** Cell dich can khoa bao mat. Neu chuoi NH (Next Hop) bi dut, chuyen giao that bai.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

RAN truyen thong hoat dong voi thuat toan tinh. Near-RT RIC cho phep toi uu thoi gian thuc вҖ” dua ra quyet dinh trong 10ms den 1s dua tren dieu kien mang song.

Day la noi AI/ML gap RAN. Hieu Near-RT RIC la can thiet cho trien khai O-RAN hien dai.

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Vuot qua do tre E2.** Vong dieu khien phai hoan thanh trong &lt;1s. Do tre mang + xu ly phai vua ngan sach.

2. **Qua tai dang ky.** Qua nhieu xApps dang ky cung du lieu. Dung gop dang ky.

3. **Hanh dong xApp xung dot.** xApp MLB va xApp MRO ra lenh xung dot. Bat giam thieu xung dot.

4. **Khong khop phien ban E2SM.** E2 node va xApp dung phien ban E2SM khac. Kiem tra tuong thich.

5. **NghбәҪn SDL.** Tat ca xApps do xo vao Redis. Toi uu mau doc/ghi, dung cache cuc bo.

6. **Thieu RAN function.** xApp dang ky chuc nang E2 node khong ho tro. Kiem tra phan hoi E2 Setup.

7. **Hanh dong dieu khien bi tu choi.** E2 node tu choi hanh dong do rang buoc. Xu ly RIC Control Failure.

8. **Phuc hoi khi xApp crash.** xApp khoi dong lai, mat trang thai dang ky. Dung subscription manager de luu tru.

## Tai sao dieu nay quan trong

Open Fronthaul hua hen tinh linh hoat da vendor. Nhung mang truyen tai phai dap ung cac yeu cau nghiem ngat — bang thong, do tre, jitter, va dong bo — neu khong he thong se that bai.

Hieu yeu cau truyen tai la can thiet cho thiet ke mang fronthaul. Lam sai, va RAN khong hoat dong.

</section>

## Ap dung

**Tom tat yeu cau truyen tai:**

| Tham so | Yeu cau | Ghi chu |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Bang thong | 10-25 Gbps/RU | Co nen |
| Do tre mot chieu | &lt;100 µs | Khong ke xu ly DU/RU |
| Jitter | &lt;1 µs | Cho timing symbol dung |
| Dong bo pha | ±1.5 µs | Tai giao dien khong khi |
| Dong bo tan so | ±50 ppb | Can SyncE |
| San sang | 99.999% | Nam chin |
| So hop toi da | 10-15 | Ngan sach PTP |

**Danh sach kiem tra thiet ke:**

```bash
# Tinh bang thong
PRBs=273; LAYERS=4; SCS=30
BW_UNCOMPRESSED=$((PRBs * 12 * 2 * 32 * 14 * SCS / 15 * LAYERS / 1000000000))
echo "Khong nen: ${BW_UNCOMPRESSED} Gbps"
# Nen (BFP 9-bit): nhan 0.6

# Ngan sach do tre (cap quang)
FIBER_KM=10
LATENCY_US=$((FIBER_KM * 5))
echo "Do tre cap quang: ${LATENCY_US} µs"

# Xac minh PTP
ptp4l -i eth0 -m
# Kiem tra offset < 100 ns

# Xac minh SyncE
synce4l -i eth0 -m
# Kiem tra tin nhan QL va SSM
```

**Yeu cau switch:**

| Tinh nang | Muc dich |
|-----------|----------|
| Cong 10/25 GbE | Bang thong |
| PTP (G.8275.1) | Dong bo pha |
| SyncE | Dong bo tan so |
| 802.1Qbu | Frame preemption |
| 802.1Qbv | Time-aware shaper |
| Cut-through switching | Do tre thap |

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Cam bay

1. **Danh gia thap bang thong.** Dung quen overhead eCPRI, nhieu carrier, nhieu sector. Kich thuoc cho dinh, khong trung binh.

2. **Bo qua jitter.** Do tre la trung binh. Jitter la phuong sai. Jitter cao gay loi timing symbol ngay khi do tre trung binh thap.

3. **PTP qua switch khong phai BC.** Moi switch phai la boundary clock (T-BC). Transparent clock (T-TC) them sai so vuot ngan sach.

4. **SyncE bi tat.** Khong co SyncE, holdover PTP khi mat GNSS xuong cap nhanh. Bat tren moi hop.

5. **Switch store-and-forward.** Cut-through switching giam do tre. Store-and-forward them do tre phu thuoc kich thuoc frame.

6. **QoS VLAN khong du.** Priority queuing giup nhung khong dam bao tinh xac dinh. TSN (Qbu/Qbv) cung cap do tre gioi han.

7. **Bao ve vong qua cham.** G.8032 &lt;50 ms on cho backhaul, khong fronthaul. Xem xet bao ve &lt;1 ms hoac dual-homing.

8. **Tinh sai khoang cach.** Do tre cap quang la 5 µs/km. Cong do tre switch moi hop. Ngan sach chat sau 10-15 km.

</section>

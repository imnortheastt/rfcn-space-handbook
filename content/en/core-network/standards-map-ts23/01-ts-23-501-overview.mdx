## Why this matters

TS 23.501 is the "bible" of 5G architecture. Every NF, every interface, every concept traces back to this document. Understanding its structure means understanding 5G.

<section class="lesson-pitfalls">

## Pitfalls

1. **Confusing with TS 23.502.** 23.501 = architecture (static). 23.502 = procedures (dynamic). Both needed.

2. **Missing service-based view.** Spec has TWO representations. SBI is the actual implementation.

3. **Ignoring annexes.** Annexes have examples and clarifications. They're informative but valuable.

4. **Version mismatch.** Rel-15, 16, 17, 18 have different features. Check your target release.

5. **Assuming completeness.** 23.501 is high-level. Protocol details are in other specs.

6. **Skipping Clause 3.** Abbreviations are dense. Keep definitions handy.

7. **Not following references.** "[N]" citations point to mandatory reading.

8. **Treating all NFs equally.** AMF, SMF, UPF are mandatory. Others may be optional.
